The Second Annual
Heron’s Nest Illustration Contest

 

The Editors and staff of The Heron’s Nest invite you to submit samples of your artwork to consider for the second annual print edition of The Heron’s Nest (Volume VIII).

The art may be in any medium, including photographs. Work intended for the cover may be in color and will be printed in color (if selected). Sectional art can be submitted in color but will be printed in black and white.

The cover theme must depict either herons or their nests. Sectional dividers need not include images of herons. Seasonal divisions should, of course, have themes appropriate to them. The other sections (overview, readers’s choice awards, memorials, special mentions, and voters’s comments) we leave open to your aesthetic sensibilities.

The dimensions of the cover will be the same as the printed Nest has been in the past (5 1/2” x 8 1/2”). The selected art may be presented entirely on the front cover or it may wrap around to the back cover (as it did for Volume VII). Some small amount of sizing may be necessary to maximize effect. If so, a proof will be sent to the winner for approval. Only original works that have not been published will be considered and all rights to the artwork will return to the artist once Volume VIII has been printed.

PLEASE SUBMIT SIX COPIES OF EACH PIECE OF ART

DEADLINE: in-hand September 1, 2006

All entries are to be sent to Christopher Herold, 816 Taft Street, Port Townsend, WA 98368. Judges will be the editors and staff of The Heron’s Nest and a professional artist yet to be selected.

Entries will not be returned without your request. If you wish your work returned, please include a SASE for that purpose.

We wish you much enjoyment in your artistic endeavors and look forward to receiving your submissions. Remember, the deadline is September 1.

In the spirit of haikai,

Christopher Herold
