spring break —
the migrant child chases
willow fluff

Nara Bauer

 

mud wasp and I
alive together —
outdoor bath

C. Avery

 

swallow bellies
grazing the water
we drink

Paul Pfleuger, Jr.

 

North Carolina —
the lush local dialect
of mockingbird song

Marianna Monaco

 

waterfall
a sad man’s face
in the bark of a laurel

Brent Partridge

 
 
 

Pale green waves
jostle ashore in twilight . . .
the throb of bullfrogs

Joan Zimmerman

 

fern
uncurling
the faintest breeze

Robbie Gamble

 

dogwoods near the stream
  a blossom
    in our jelly jar

Kay F. Anderson

 

the dark folds
of a greening mountain —
my sister’s locked diary

D. Claire Gallagher

 

mountain pass —
each valley a different shade
of spring

Nara Bauer
