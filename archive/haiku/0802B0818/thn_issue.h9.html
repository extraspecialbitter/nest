this evening
with no dog barking . . .
autumn wind

israel lopez balan

 

decayed wet leaves —
on the storm drain
a blanched worm

Raffael De Gruttola

 

Calder sculpture;
a single brown leaf tumbling
from the sky

D. F. Tweney

 

autumn wind —
from next door’s garden
the sound of hide-and-seek

John Kinory

 

full moon
my monthly loss
for words

Yvonne Cabalona

 
 
 

morning fog —
the dog keeps some slack
in his leash

Darrell Byrd

 

late fall
one by one parachutists
return to earth

LeRoy Gorman

 

Christmas shopping —
my daughter’s lip rolls
into her mother’s pout

Curtis Dunlap

 

fading light —
his voice in the crowd
of shadows

Lorin Ford

 

empty page
the dog’s gray muzzle
on my knee

Deb Baker
