Ellen Compton

baled hay
left in the field
a cricket’s ragged chirp

Jack Barry

 

last light
an ant swims
in the hummingbird feeder

Sabine Miller

 

summer stars
we pick out
our house

Victor Ortiz

 

a moonless night
the old car’s muffler
leaves sparks in its wake

Barry Goodman

 

kite shop —
a red dragonfly
flits in and out

Francis Masat

 
 
 

one more game
of shirts vs. skins
summer dusk

Collin Barber

 

in the pleat
of a morning glory
a firefly’s light

Emily Romano

 

midnight
some of the sounds
are insects

Quendryth Young

 

rising sun
the shadow three times bigger
than the bird

Brenda J. Gannam

 

red leaf
I return it
to the shore wind

Ellen Compton
