   Homeless shelter —
the glimmer of sunlight
     on broken glass

Edward Zuk

 

autumn leaves
the creak
in my left knee

Stephen Peters

 

bitter wind —
a crane lands among
thronging cranes

Allan Burns

 

the house-fronts
changing color
in the rain

Michael McClintock

 

autumn leaves
a religious pamphlet
left on the bridge

Rick Tarquinio

 
 
 

dreary day
a black walnut
gnawed white

H. Gene Murtha

 

storm front —
the wind gauge pointing
directly at me

Lane Parker

 

November rain . . .
embracing the weight
of my sister’s urn

Connie Donleycott

 

sweet-grass braids
we bury Grandmother
without her wig

Andrew Riutta

 

fish ladder —
patches of moss
on graffiti

D. Claire Gallagher
