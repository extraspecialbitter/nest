Ruth Yarrow

clear whistle
of the white-throated sparrow —
evening star

Ruth Holzer

 

half moon
something slips into the pond
besides me

Peter Yovu

 

river rock —
the folded wings
of a damselfly

Carolyn Hall

 

stone causeway
the creek’s summer flow
rearranging pebbles

Sue Mill

 

Silent woods . . .
a growing awareness of
the need to breathe

George Swede

 
 
 

mother and daughter
each in her own thoughts
fireflies

Agnes Eva Savich

 

waking
in the fetal position
. . . the river’s hum

Paul Pfleuger, Jr.

 

waterfall view —
soba noodles slipping
from my chopsticks

D. Claire Gallagher

 

noon heat
a swallow just ahead
of its shadow

Tony Beyer

 

wind upriver —
undersides of cottonwood leaves
rippling silver

Ruth Yarrow
