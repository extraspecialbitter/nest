open casket–
the absence
of his cologne

Amy Hartl Sherman

 

loneliness
a hint of salt
in the rain

Linda Robeck

 

needle-cushioned path–
only when he hops
the red toad

A. C. Missias

 

Maine–
granite poking up
through the lawn

Paul Watsky

 

slight arc
of the stepping stones
summer moon

Burnell Lippy

 
 
 

spring rain–
Dad’s name misspelled
in the obituary

Kathy Lippard Cobb

 

mountain air
a caterpillar reaches
the twig’s end

an’ya

 

warm rain
in the center of the tree stump
a black hole

Zinovy Vayman

 
peony buds–
can an ant
relax?
John Stevenson

 

tiring heat–
the newspaper story continues
on another page

Gary Hotham
