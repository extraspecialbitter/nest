In Memory of Robert Spiess
October 16, 1921 – March 13, 2002

On Wednesday, March 13, 2002, a great man passed away.

      the field's evening fog–
              quietly the hound comes
                      to fetch me home

                                            Bob Spiess
Cottage of the Wild Plum
Modern Haiku Press, 1991

We at The Heron's Nest send our condolences to Bob Spiess's family and his many friends. We also wish to express profound gratitude for the outstanding contribution Bob made to the world community of haiku poets. We shall remember him as a kind man, a mentor, and a friend. Bob dedicated his life to the study and enjoyment of haiku. While doing so, he provided us poets with Modern Haiku, for which he was the Editor-in-Chief for twenty-five years. Modern Haiku is at once a splendid place to share our insightful moments, and an outstanding resource through which to educate ourselves. The value of Bob's nurturing influence is incalculable. We shall miss him more than words can express, yet we are blessed–his legacy will be with us for many, many years to come.

– Christopher Herold
