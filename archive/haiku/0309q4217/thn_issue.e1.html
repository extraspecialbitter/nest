naia
Heron's Nest Award

Carol Purington

Sandra Simpson


 
 

spring thunder–
from the center of a pine stump
a pine

naia

 
 

 
 

winter solstice
the road narrows
into one-way

 
 
 

summer's end–
beachstore buckets on dusty shelves
anyoldhow

Carol Purington

 

Sandra Simpson
