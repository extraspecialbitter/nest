starry sky
the honks of wild geese
rise from the quarry

Stephen Amor

 

cold moon slides
up the long window
shadow of her hand

Lenard D. Moore

 

bare winter prairie...
a little smoke rises
from an empty field

Bruce Ross

 

autumn wind
a tiny spider husk
spins in the moonlight

John Barlow

 

a cottonwood stump
brought in by the tide–
sparrow song

Cindy Zackowitz

 
 
 

solitary walk
dragging the stars
through pines

Odd G. Aksnes

 

first frost–
a daddy-long-legs
skims over bright leaves

Billie Wilson

 

prairie arroyo
half-grown bison bulls
corral the trucks

Elizabeth Howard

 
December beach
not a sign to be seen
of Christmas
Naomi Wakan

 

churchyard manger
knots in the plywood backs
of the wise men

Michael Ketchek
