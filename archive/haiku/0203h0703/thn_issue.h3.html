kitchen table
wild flower bouquet

Dean Summers

 

birdbath
the rising sun
splashed with bluejays

Debi Bender

the plodding turtle
curves its neck

Matthew Louvière

 

along with the tide
a ribbon of kelp
fishes itself ashore

David E. LeCount

 

bamboo in snow–
but for a Sung painter's art

their golden petals
taken by wind

Mary Fran Meer

for the shovel
winter dawn
John Stevenson

the yellow kitchen
even more welcoming
