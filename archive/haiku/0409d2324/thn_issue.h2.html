low sun
behind the churchyard
piles of earth

Paul Wigelius

 

early dusk–
an obituary taped
in a used book

Mark Brooks

 

full moon
the sidewalk curves
around tree roots

Philomene Kocher

 

its whole body
cawing–
the crow

Hilary Tann

 

a dried leaf
slips from her journal
new year's eve

Pamela Miller Ness

 
 
 

borrowed novel–
a bookmark left
near the end

Sonia Cristina Coman

 

séance–
a white moth flutters
above our clasped hands

Emily Romano

 

All Souls' Eve
limbs of the sycamore
through the fog

Jim Kacian

 

fading light . . .
the faint smell of camphor
on old love letters

J. B. Leong

 

twenty below
the Milky Way
lined up with the river

Burnell Lippy
