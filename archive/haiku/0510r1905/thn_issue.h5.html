scent of lemon
the glint of a sunbeam
on polished pews

Sue Mill

 

home from the lawyer
my old soft shimmy
and a bowl of huckleberries

Elizabeth Howard

 

first light
filling the orchard —
squeak of the pump

H. F. Noyes

 

what my words can’t explain —
the autumn sun
on your back

paul m.

 

quiet afternoon —
leaves on her desk
arranged by colour

Michael Dylan Welch

 
 
 

Creaking gate —
the crows evenly spaced
between the headstones

Rebecca Lilly

 

summer night . . .
    slowly he disappears
        into cicada song

Naia

 

the angle
of the fruit picker’s ladder
autumn light

Tom Painting

 

continuing heat
how does the maple know
that it’s autumn?

Yvonne Cabalona

 

holding her close—
the pulse
of cricket song

Brian Gierat
