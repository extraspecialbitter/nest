Cherie Hunter Day

 

Christmas Eve —
bits of a price sticker
stuck to my finger

Michael Dylan Welch

 

trespassing —
a swirl of leaves
around my ankles

Carolyne Rohrig

 

starry night
the queue for skates
moves slowly

Marcus Larsson

 

midnight sun —
we leave our sauna quarrel
in the icy lake

Nancy Stewart Smith

 
 
 

cold rain
     slicks the café tables
her lips at his ear

Dave Russo

 

a girl wandering
in the year-end crowd
her white cane

Hiroshi Tokui

 

dogwood winter
the waterfall plummets
into a ring of ice

Elizabeth Howard

 

onto my face
snow that started falling
before the stars appeared

Peter Yovu

 

deep winter
where have I lost
the third hubcap

Joann Klontz
