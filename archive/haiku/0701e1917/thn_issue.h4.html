evening star —
fold upon fold
the quiet blue hills

Mary Lee McClure

 

day at the zoo —
the elephant’s shadow
in a small place

Gary Hotham

 

sunshine on the canal
an old nun stops
to empty her shoes

Colo Buchanan

 

outdoor café
the dog takes interest
in my interest

Bruce Ross

 

cool dusk
the tide finds
the sandcastle

Tony Beyer

 
 
 

early summer
the children’s list
of what they’ll bring

Marcus Larsson

 

dry season —
rock paintings
by a vanished people

Sandra Simpson

 

August afternoon —
behind the poetry books
a dry apple core

Ruth Holzer

 

new paperback —
the sun sets
without me

David Giacalone

 

bright stars
campfire coffee
full of grounds

Charlie Close
