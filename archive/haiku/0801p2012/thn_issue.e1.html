Editors’ Choices

 

summer morning
squeak
of the bicycle seat
Steven Thunell

 

chill wind —
the heart of an oak
leaves the chimney
Robert Bauer

 

appointment calendar
a coffee ring joins
one day to the next
LeRoy Gorman
