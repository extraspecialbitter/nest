we both speak

beside me

winter stars . . .
I learn to paint
empty spaces

the soap bar
razor thin

snow day . . .

sending prayer slips
with the ebb tide

a grandchild draws
my weary smile

over the snowy fields —
Orion

Tomislav Maretic

 

first snow —
against my will
the beauty

Mary Lee McClure

 

leaf-fall
for a moment
the cat all kitten

Yvonne Cabalona
