On the Road
as I turn, each yellowed page
breaks free

Sue Stanford

 

foghorns . . .
the ginkgo’s last leaves
fall with the snow

Connie Donleycott

 

winter commute
the train’s momentum
in the small of my back

Matthew Paul

 

filtered through pine
the pygmy owl’s staccato —
flecks of moonlight

Ruth Yarrow

 

winter night
I sip ginger tea
from my sister’s cup

Audrey Downey

 
 
 

        cold snap
hotdog vendor dancing
    to warm his feet

Adelaide McLeod

 

first snow
one child
wakes the others

John Stevenson

 

New Year’s Eve
the old janitor
wrings out his mop

Nara Bauer

 

New Year’s Eve
one side of the mare
dusted with snow

Linda Jeannette Ward

 

Orion overhead
our last kiss of the year
is the first of the next

Angelee Deodhar
