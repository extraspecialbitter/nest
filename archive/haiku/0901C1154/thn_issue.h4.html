spring dawn —
the beggar dumps a spider
from her cup

Bob Lucky

 

Sacré-Cœur
a ladder almost up to
a stained glass window

Bruce Ross

 

school dismissed
the cemetery shortcut
comes alive

LeRoy Gorman

 

summer solstice
a little sunfish
puts up a fight

William Cullen, Jr.

 

a butterfly
so long at my window
summer dusk

Carolyn Hall

 
 
 

lilacs in bloom . . .
the lecturer drones on
about “End-of-Life”

Jennifer Soule

 

Memorial Day —
a graveyard full
of Old Glories

Tom Tico

 

gentle days
the tethered dinghy
drifts round

Greg Piko

 

solstice shadows
the mockingbird’s breast
a study in blues

Jennie Townsend

 

summer solstice . . .
the sound of a door closing
at dusk

Marianna Monaco
