Margarita Engle

jasmine tendrils —
waiting
for my daughter’s call

Nathalie Buckland

 

dawn . . .
the rooster for sacrifice
calls in the temple

K. Ramesh

 

Dry creek bed
the seven stepping stones
to the other side

George Swede

 

licking raspberry stains
from my palm —
the heat

Margaret Chula

 

clear day . . .
a painted turtle
takes the plunge

Hilary Tann

 
 
 

a raven’s cry . . .
the fading lines
of the petroglyph

Timothy Hawkes

 

left on the shrine
the stone that tumbled
from the cliff

C. Avery

 

stepping stones
               she moves
                     a little closer

Yu Chang

 

blue summer morning . . .
the egret returns
to the guppies’ pond

Janelle Barrera

 

summer sky
a damselfly stirs
the blue air

Margarita Engle
