bare branches
a coyote howls
into the wind

Liz Crane

 

waiting in the room
with the plaster skeleton —
early winter

Ruth Holzer

 

death watch —
clouds connect the sky
one end to the other

Gary Hotham

 

cemetery garden
rain falls on
plastic leaves

Sherry Weaver-Smith

 

biting cold —
moonlight on the nail heads
of the old fence

Cindy Zackowitz

 
 
 

we pass
humming different tunes
winter moon

paul m.

 

long illness
unfolding someone’s
dog-eared pages

Ron Moss

 

winter night . . .
father’s last words
never come

Irene Golas

 

feather in the grass . . .
I grieve for a friend
I never met

Sabine Miller

 

hail melt
a lizard skin shimmers
on the broken wall

Jo McInerney
