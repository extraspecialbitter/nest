Philip Miller

the crouch
of a grasshopper

Alice Frampton

 

Family quarrel
the breakfast tartness
of fresh quince jam

Joan Zimmermann

 

parking lot crow —
the same complaints
every morning

Priscilla VanValkenburgh

 

autumn river
the old couple walks
not holding hands

Neil Muscott

 
 
 

Indian summer
the drawing teacher draws
between classes

Marcus Larsson

 

clouded moon
I let the machine
pick up his call

Aurora Antonovic

 

early to the station
ducks fly overhead
in pairs

paul m.

 

they speak again
if only of the weather
clearing sky

Greg Piko

 

ginko
to learn of the pine . . .
sticky fingers

Joan Murphy
