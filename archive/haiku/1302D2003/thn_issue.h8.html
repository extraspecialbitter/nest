Lorin Ford

Mathew V. Spano

Quendryth Young

Marilyn Appl Walker

Charles Trumbull

Dan Grace

John Hawk

temple bells . . .
the clink of coins
in ceramic pots

Wende Skidmore DuFlon

 

I check once again
for any missed calls —
intermittent rain

Charles Trumbull

 

crewelwork
the musty scent
of geraniums

Pat Tompkins

 

high sun
ants to the top
of the okra stalk

Marilyn Appl Walker

 

august rain
the one bitter almond
in a whole bag

Dan Grace

 
 
 

nailing roof iron
with my father’s hammer
. . . the echoes

Lorin Ford

 

attic light
dust rising from the dark
in the magic hat

Mathew V. Spano

 

ebb tide
mangrove roots hold
a downy feather

Quendryth Young

 

dentist’s window
birds picking out
the sunflower seeds

Charles Trumbull

 

low tide
dusk collecting
in the shells

John Hawk
