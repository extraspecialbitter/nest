third day of snow
neighborhood kids build
a rhinoceros

Christopher Herold

 

winter morning
two butterflies
warm the garden

P K Padhy

 

narcissus . . .
my heartbeat races
towards its end

Jim Kacian

 

a setback
in her treatment
frost-tipped lavender

Carolyn Hall

 

new minister . . .
gutters guide a spring rain
back to the earth

Kirsty Karkow

 
 
 

March snowmen —
Picassos appearing
on front lawns

John Soules

 

miles of silence
a chill on my thigh
when he changes gears

Tanya McDonald

 

snowmelt
the caw of the crow returns
to the hickory

Merrill Ann Gonzales

 

snowdrops surround
an empty snail shell
pale day moon

Beverley George

 

tsunami . . .
untangling
my knotted rosary

Patrick Sweeney
