Cindy Zackowitz

vendor on the beach–
from hand to hand
the spinning pinwheel

K. Ramesh

 

the crab points skyward
with its remaining claw–
crane’s shadow

Donald J. Lanska

 

evening light
following the anchor line
into the sea

Cindy Zackowitz

 

rain stopped
the sunflower drips
into its shadow

William Cullen, Jr.

 

buried in a coat
he never wore–
autumn equinox

Penelope Greenwell

 
 
 

gray morning sky
a herring gull drops a mollusk
onto rocks

Emily Romano

 

afternoon swim
in his hair
the scent of fish

Jeanne Marie Booth

 

factory whistle
blue morning glories
                on the flag pole

Nina Wicker

 
Labor Day
an apple bough bent
to the ground
Carolyn Hall

 

autumn equinox–
fresh tooth marks
in the old birch

Cindy Zackowitz
