autumn creek
each leaf shadow haloed
in sunlight

Christopher Patchel

 

last cast
a fisherman’s breath
becomes river mist

Matt Morden

 

moon shadow
an owl plucks a mouse
from rising water

Stephen Amor

 

afternoon burble
the yin yang of two beef tongues
in a pot

Anna Tambour

 

hospital ward–
I try to isolate
her special scent

H. F. Noyes

 
 
 

still water
the crayfish slips under
the sky

Sabine Miller

 

moon almost full–
the blackness flows
downstream

Yvonne Cabalona

 

falling leaves–
the same old man returning
to the same old park bench

Robert Kusiolek

 
childhood home
      twilight
            as I arrive
John Stevenson

 

wish made . . .
concentric ripples
spread away

Robert Major
