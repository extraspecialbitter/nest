William Males

 

slow train home
a man skims Ecclesiastes
on his kindle

Jon Baldwin

 

that yellow bird’s back
how we end up
where we do

Robert Epstein

 

it must’ve happened before
a drop of spring rain
in my green tea

Patrick Sweeney

 

spring sun
I wait for the snake
to move

Hilary Tann

Christopher Patchel

Bill Cooper

that don’t fit

John Soules

 

so lightly
a morning rainbow disappears
in the rice paddy

Motoko Amatsuji

 

kite festival —
the startling red
of a cockscomb
