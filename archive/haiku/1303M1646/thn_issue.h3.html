a crow cleans its beak
on an ice-covered bough
early morning sun

Christopher Herold

 

her illness
beyond our care
winter birds

Laurence Stacey

 

deep snow
hoses curled
under the sundeck

Joanna M. Weston

 

morning prayers
the suet cake
pecked thin

Jennie Townsend

 

everything
we have in common
plum blossom rain

Polona Oblak

 
 
 

a nursing home blanket
over all her sharp edges —
midwinter

Jennifer Gomoll Popolis

 

the difference
a sparrow makes —
bare branches

Bill Kenney

 

deep winter —
a seal skin tacked
to my neighbor’s door

Cindy Zackowitz

 

new moon —
my friend coming out
of her dark phase

Mary Ahearn

 

do we all
mark time
with blossoms

Yu Chang
