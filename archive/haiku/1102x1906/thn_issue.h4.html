Chris Bays

Carolyn Hall

Pat Benedict

Bruce Ross

Christopher Herold

Jordan Hawkes

leaning trees
almost touch across the creek
a dove’s flight display

Allan Burns

 

sultry night
the rhythm of oars
stroking the lake

Bill Kenney

 

clipping tags
from luggage . . .
summer’s end

Chris Bays

 

rolling in
with the morning fog
seabird on a wave

Pat Benedict

 

mist into rain
the drone of a small plane
blends with our chanting

Christopher Herold

 
 
 

headwaters
the dipper’s shadow
follows its call

Allan Burns

 

out of names —
we sit silent in the dark
among the stars

Don Holt

 

autumn dusk
the gates of the lock
close behind us

Carolyn Hall

 

Point Reyes lighthouse
how the deep note travels
on the water

Bruce Ross

 

school bell . . .
noticing her
for the first time

Jordan Hawkes
