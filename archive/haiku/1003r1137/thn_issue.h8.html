tire marks
on the neighborhood street
evening heat

Lenard D. Moore

 

hot summer day
the shape of a bicycle
that’s been run over

Carlos Colón

 

one tree
downhill from the others
the limit of shadows

Jennie Townsend

 

watermelon moon
she pedals slower
closer to home

C. Avery

 

drive-thru exit
a one-legged gull waits
to take my order

LeRoy Gorman

the train tracks

Matthew Cariello

prayer flags fade
on temple trees

Edith Bartholomeusz

 

summer waters . . .
the slack
in each line

Chad Lee Robinson

a water dragon’s tail
dangles in the pool

Quendryth Young

 

midnight —
another ping from
the empty pop can

Mike Montreuil
