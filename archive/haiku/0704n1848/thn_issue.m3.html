Memorial Poems from the Heron’s Nest

 

Ferris Gilli

Paul MacNeil

Peggy Willis Lyles

Paul David Mena

Robert Gilliland

Christopher Herold

another leaf drifts
from the landmark oak
wren song

Ferris Gilli

 

turning tide
the river goes
from gray to blue

Peggy Willis Lyles

 

daybreak
birdsong glides
from oak to elm

Robert Gilliland

 
 
 

my flight delayed
he polishes the barware
with a flourish

Paul MacNeil

 

silence
on Telegraph Hill —
a shooting star

Paul David Mena

 

sundown . . .
the mason jars
empty again

Christopher Herold
