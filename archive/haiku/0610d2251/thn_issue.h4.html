evensong —
from the flooded field
a fullthroated frog

Angelee Deodhar

 

thunderhead —
a plume of smoke
from the old salt’s mouth

Gary Steinberg

 

summer solstice
the measuring tape reels back
into its case

Carolyn Hall

 

a hawk’s shrill
above the sunlit meadow —
empty mailbox

Francis Masat

 

between a rock
and a hard place . . .
the oncologist’s fish

Kay F. Anderson

 
 
 

singing gondolier
the passengers’
fixed smiles

Kay Grimnes

 

the long day —
a mason drizzles mortar
between flagstones

Robert Bauer

 

Berlin Wall
a smooth stone
in my pocket

H. Gene Murtha

 

doctor’s waiting room —
each patient examined
by a fly

Mike Blottenberger

 

still alive!
a distant neighbor’s
summer-blooming maple

George Dorsty
