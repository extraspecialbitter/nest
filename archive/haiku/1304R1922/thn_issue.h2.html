Ann K. Schwader

George Stratton

Kirsty Karkow

PMF Johnson

Raffael de Gruttola

Joyce Clement

Ron Moss

LeRoy Gorman

Margaret Dornaus

George Swede

the almost
of crabapple buds
hazy sky

Ann K. Schwader

 

I will not leave —
apple blossoms collect
on my shoulders

Kirsty Karkow

 

incense for remembrance
water trickles down
the mountain side

Raffael de Gruttola

 

matching exhales
with the sleeping dog . . .
bone coloured mist

Ron Moss

 

soapsuds —
mother tells me how
she’d like to die

Margaret Dornaus

 
 
 

construction site
yellow ribbons
outline the future

George Stratton

 

puddle
in the umbrella
morning sun

PMF Johnson

 

childless
I measure time
by how the willow grows

Joyce Clement

 

Easter fog lifting
a blowfly rises
from roadkill

LeRoy Gorman

 

cemetery bees
her last diploma
made of marble

George Swede
