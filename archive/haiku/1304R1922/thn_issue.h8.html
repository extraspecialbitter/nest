home for Christmas
the tree losing needles
on fake snow

Gary Hotham

 

as I turn the page
Napoleon leaves Moscow
fading winter light

J. Zimmerman

 

year’s first moon
the widow turns
her mattress

Nika

 

winter night —
I dream
in monochrome

Sanjukta Asopa

 

finally, good news —
the reflection of my face
inside a tea bubble

Marshall Bood

 
 
 

dumpster
the iridescence
of starlings

Bill Kenney

 

snowy night
drinking tea
from my hometown

Chen-ou Liu

 

over old ground . . .
a dunnock’s search
in winter shrubs

Thomas Powell

 

low tide
gull and gannet shadows
pick shells

Patricia Prime

 

spring peepers
raindrops stipple the creek
in chorus

Michele Root-Bernstein
