Jeffrey Woodward

turning leaves
he flips a penny
into the sun

James Chessing

 

a medevac helicopter
passes low —
scent of autumn leaves

Cindy Zackowitz

 

fading light . . .
pink-footed geese wade
into the sun

Claire Everett

 

chimney smoke
the long straight rows
of cornfield stubble

Barbara Snow

 

a lullaby
turning to whisper . . .
moonrise

Michele L. Harvey

 
 
 

two dragonflies
riding the screen door
autumn deepens

Burnell Lippy

 

ghost of
a white gazebo
night rain

Ann K. Schwader

 

counting the lines
on my neck —
magpie song

Sandra Simpson

 

cornflakes
soaked with milk
all the leaves have fallen

LeRoy Gorman

 

mallet to chisel,
chisel to stone . . .
the north wind

Jeffrey Woodward
