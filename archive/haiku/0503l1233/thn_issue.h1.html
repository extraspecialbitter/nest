cold sky
my boots grind stones
in the dry wash

Victor Ortiz

 

clanging buoy —
how many voices
does the sea have?

William Cullen, Jr.

 

cold rain —
tips of new tulips
rim the muskeg

Billie Wilson

 

Spring thaw . . .
          the ewe wants to be petted
                in all the old places

vincent tripi

 

a door-to-door salesman
lengthens his stride —
the scent of wisteria

Sandra Simpson

 
 
 

winter beach
the glint of white
in the raven’s eye

Carolyn Hall

 

the cattails
lose their heads

Tom Painting

 

night rain
the new puppy suckles
in his sleep

Maria Steyn

 

one white lily
in the koi pond —
Mother’s Day

Joann Klontz

 

the observatory dome
opens wide —
                     moths

Michael McClintock
