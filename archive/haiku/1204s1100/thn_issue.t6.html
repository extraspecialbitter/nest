the first night
the bright star's
below the horizon

Allan Burns

 

news of her death
on the night table
the book unsigned

Roberta Beary

 

hummingbird flight
the swiftest movements
are invisible

Margarita Engle

 

the last
stair step —
breathing the sky

Eduard Tara

 

 
 
 

an empty chair
beside me —
crickets' requiem

Fay Aoyagi

 

softly at first
on a swollen wind
voices in the rain

Ron Moss

 

harp music . . .
wild pear blossoms
in the teacher's window

Maria Steyn

 

ebb tide beach
so much to be treasured
left behind

johnny baranski
