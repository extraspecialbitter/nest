dusty path
the summer taste
of raspberries

Lynne Steel

 

next to each other
a boat full of people
one full of pelicans

Bruce Ross

 

glassy pond
the rattling blue dive
of a kingfisher

Elizabeth Howard

 

desert twilight
a packrat peers into
my empty boot

Darrell Byrd

 

  cool summer night–
       a full moon
frames the bird feeder

Michael Dylan Welch

 
 
 

a sweat bee
stings my hand–
summer heat

Curtis Dunlap

 

becalmed;
a blue butterfly
clings to the sail

Emily Romano

 

twilight lake
a merganser pulls silver water
into dark

Ruth Yarrow

 
saguaro blossoms
in moonlight
the first bat’s shadow
Naomi Y. Brown

 

distant thunder
the old dog growls
in his sleep

Renate Sander-Regier
