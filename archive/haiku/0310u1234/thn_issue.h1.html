Alice Frampton

 

autumn stillness
she straightens a shelf
of paper cranes

paul m.

 

garage sale
unraveled flowers
on the kimono

in a puddle

the path

Sabine Miller

the third-grade classroom

Peggy Lyles

 

outside view

Gillena Cox

twilight in the park

      from all the dark places
Bruce Kennedy

 

harvest moon
the scarecrow
wears a tie
