he whistles
the latest love song

Sandra Simpson

what to tell her . . .
bruised mint

Jennifer Gomoll Popolis

pulling off
cockleburs

I turn the glider cushion
to its clean side

of a stream

in the dead man’s house

I absorb the stillness
in the trees
