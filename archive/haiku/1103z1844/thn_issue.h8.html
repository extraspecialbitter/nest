the darkness
of long-empty stables —
Quaker Meetinghouse

Barry George

 

three-quarter moon
the windmill rusted
into silence

Lynne Steel

 

Sunset and moonrise —
if only I could learn
to be faithful

Joan Zimmerman

 

October frost
the tread of the landlord
on crisp brown leaves

Michael McClintock

 

Grandmother
the stoop
in her shadow

André Surridge

 
 
 

soldiers coming home . . .
three wasps circle where
the nest used to hang

George Swede

 

click-clack
of the bead curtain —
the sway of her hips

Sandra Simpson

 

a Sioux tunic
embroidered with elk teeth
behind smudged glass

Pat Tompkins

 

thin marsh ice
my father attempts
an embrace

Glenn Coats

 

a sparkler fizzles
into darkness
year’s end

Collin Barber
