Tributes to Paul O. Williams

 

Walden —
      took his skipping stone with him
            when he went

 — vincent tripi

sudden gust
a mimosa blossom floats
closer to the clouds

 — Carlos Colón

clouded moon
praising the maker
of the fish sauce

 — Michael McClintock

where the trout vanished ripples expand through the pond

 — Marian Olson

Walden absorbs
a fallen oak —
the cold, damp hush

 — Peggy Willis Lyles
