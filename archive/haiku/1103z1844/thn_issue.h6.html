stars begin to fade . . .
so much music
from unseen birds

Tyrone McDonald

 

slow train journey
         a gull’s shadow
                takes the lead

Nola Borrell

 

worn out day —
rocks the river left
on top

Gary Hotham

 

midsummer
a speedboat slices
the lake in half

Carmen Sterba

 

scorching heat —
the shrilling of cicadas
pierces me

Claudia Brefeld

 
 
 

nameless falls
a water ouzel
at rainbow’s end

Grant D. Savage

 

dead man’s curve —
a screech owl veers
toward the river

Curtis Dunlap

 

after each
turn of the page
the fly

Robert Epstein

 

thunderclap —
the sun tea
darkens

Jeff Stillman

 

secret campsite
chilled beer clinks
in midstream

Ron Moss
