handshake —
our sleeves touched
by the autumn wind

Dietmar Tauchner

 

drifting hawk
soccer players surge
down the field

Richard Alan Bunch

 

my solitary life
a path worn around
the lotus pond

Lynne Steel

 

night winds
rake the pond leaves —
wobbly moon

H. F. Noyes

 

so suddenly winter
the colors
in her pill box

Carolyn Hall

 
 
 

a stiffness in the joints
shrunken tomatoes
piled in the turnrow

J. C. Herring

 

rain so heavy
it calms the water
autumn evening

Jim Kacian

 

cold drizzle . . .
one of the sparrows
sits apart

Robert Gilliland

 

frosted windows —
my weight
still on the gym scale

Michael Dylan Welch

 

pond ice
the fixed eyes
of the decoy

Tom Painting
