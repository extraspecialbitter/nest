Giselle Maya

Carolyn Hall

Terri L. French

Alan S. Bridges

Rita Odeh

Bob Lucky

Alan Summers

Ignatius Fay

pond shimmer
there not there
     grebes

Gene Murtha
Buena, New Jersey

 

early spring
the honey pot
nearly empty

Giselle Maya
St. Martin de Castillon, France

 

goose crossing
the old pick-up's
broken horn

Terri L. French
Huntsville, Alabama

 

valley stillness—
a butterfly passes through
an empty log

Rita Odeh
Nazareth, Israel

 

fading stars
a hedgehog haphazards
its way back home

Alan Summers
Bradford on Avon, England

 

 
 

filtered sunlight
where the forest thins
daffodils

Andy Burkhart
Cincinnati, Ohio

 

her gray hair
gone blonde—
first day of spring

Carolyn Hall
San Francisco, California

 

fiddlehead fern
the way it rolls
off the tongue

Alan S. Bridges
Littleton, Massachusetts

 

sunset
flash of starlings
in the acacia

Bob Lucky
Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

 

day lilies
waking to find
it wasn't a dream

Ignatius Fay
Sudbury, Ontario, Canada
