early spring . . .
specks from the sky
settle near the river

Carol Raisfeld
Atlantic Beach, New York

 

the robin's song
garlic shoots
lift the soil

Dan Grace
Sheffield, South Yorkshire, United Kingdom

 

daylight saving
the neighbor's pregnancy
begins to show

Cherie Hunter Day
Cupertino, California

 

butterbur
the rabbits explore
a little further

John Barlow
Ormskirk, England

 

Stuffed in a bus
The scent of spring
In a woman's musk

Jack Galmitz
Elmhurst, New York
 
 

inside the bud
all the reasons
to believe

Claudette Russell
Hampton, Connecticut

 

the swell
before the river splits
into another name

Jon Baldwin
Ramsgate, England

 

from the far bank
the two-note squeaks of coot chicks
strobe the river

Matthew Paul
London, England

 

outdoor wall
banyan shoot curls
in my shadow

Suhit Kelkar
Maharashtra, India

 

in the stream
a lone fisherman
that once was me

Joseph M. Kusmiss
Sanbornton, New Hampshire
