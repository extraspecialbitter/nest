Marshall Bood

Adjei Agyei-Baah

Johnnie Johnson Hafernik

Joyce Clement

Ruth Yarrow

Helen Buckingham

Urszula Wielanowska

rustle of palm leaves . . .
fishermen play cards
in the boat's shade

K. Ramesh
Chennai, India

 

roadside walk
a beggar stretches
leg as hand

Adeji Agyei-Baah
Kumasi, Ghana

 

Fiddlers' festival
the bull frogs'
uneven harmony

Peg McAuley Byrd
Madison, New Jersey

 

alone
with Dad's old songs
another night of fireflies

Joyce Clement
Bristol, Connecticut

 

    adonis blue . . .
  searching in vain
among the heathcliffs

Helen Buckingham
Bristol, Avon, England

 
 
 

summer afternoon—
the saxophone busker
takes a drag

Marshall Bood
Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada

 

tornado watch
cricket songs carried
from tall grass

Johnnie Johnson Hafernik
San Francisco, California

 

Gypsy violin . . .
the wheat harvest
sparkling with fireflies

Tatjana Debeljački
Užice, Serbia

 

mountain pass wind
whistles through my pack frame—
a pika answers

Ruth Yarrow
Seattle, Washington

 

crescent moon
quiet lullaby
from the rocker

Urszula Wielanowska
Kielce, Poland
