John Hawk

glare ice
the old dog
pees on the moon

Grant D. Savage
Ottowa, Ontario, Canada

 

sun-bright morning
dragons of factory smoke
dissolved by wind

Christopher Herold
Port Townsend, Washington

 

after all the things
that have gone wrong–
plum blossoms

Sanjukta Asopa
Karnataka, India

 

I find myself
losing the trail . . .
greening woods

Kirsty Karkow
Waldoboro, Maine

 

spring cleaning–
my daughter finds the key
to the music box

Ola Lindberg
Ystad, Sweden

 
 
 

wind blown snow
a ewe's cry
in the night

Angela Terry
Lake Forest Park, Washington

 

mild winter
the shore ice
releases its litter

Hilary Tann
Schuylerville, New York

 

measuring a life
from crocus
to crocus

Beth McFarland
Karlsruhe, Germany

 

songbirds returning . . .
the tunes my father
would whistle

Francine Banwarth
Dubuque, Iowa

 

trail frog
the children
jump back

John Hawk
Columbus, Ohio
