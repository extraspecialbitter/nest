first day of school
summer’s change
in a canning jar

Don Miller

 

waterfall
near the end of dry season
a black groove in the stone

Brent Partridge

 

this time
I don’t kill the wasp
summer’s end

she speaks of taking
one more long trip

on home plate

the marks on a hay bale
where it was bound

a sunbeam divides the melon

Keiko Izawa

 

new coolness —
the mushroom’s
steep tilt
