harvest moon
the small puddle

Jay Haskins

no hop left
in the grasshopper

David Gershator

 

highland lake —
burnt button grass
on both sides of the moon

Ron Moss

 

day moon —
another armful
of shriveled corn

the scarecrow dressed
in white linen

full moon —
all our sounds
are vowels

Chad Lee Robinson

the river changes
its tune

Harriot West

the farmer sands a splinter
on a buggy spoke

Robert Bauer

we need to be reminded
to let the young win

Marcus Larsson

 

barn loft
my children scatter wheat grains
grandfather heaped up
