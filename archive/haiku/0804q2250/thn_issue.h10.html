Matthew Paul

 

I don’t know
a soul at this picnic —
      damselflies

Carolyn Hall

 

second wedding
the fragrance of petals
torn from the rose

Michele Root-Bernstein

 

cicadas —
my strength flows
into the day’s heat

Merrill Ann Gonzales

 

making love . . .
the meadow
in my pockets

in the knot garden

of a white peacock

Joan Zimmerman

 

climbing through
a haze of mayflies
summer moon

Chris Eichenberger

 

one extra push
on the oak tree swing
suppertime
