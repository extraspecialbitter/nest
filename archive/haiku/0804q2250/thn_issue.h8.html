Spring Day
the cat returns with cobwebs
in his whiskers

Maria Steyn

 

summer cabin —
I do away
with the curtains

Audrey Downey

 

lunch time
the treecutter finds a tree
for shade

LeRoy Gorman

 

sunny snag
a pair of turtles slide
into thick silt

Stephen Engleman

 

heat wave —
  crayfish holes
      incline to the north

George Dorsty

 
 
 

boundary feud —
picking the blackberry
that breaks the web

Matt Morden

 

from deep in the forest
a haunting birdsong
sung just once

Billie Wilson

 

ancient sequoia
straw hat
in my hand

D. Claire Gallagher

 

marsh lightning
the tree’s blossoms open
into egrets

Martin Gottlieb Cohen

 

stepping stones
across the stream
your hand small in mine

John Kinory
