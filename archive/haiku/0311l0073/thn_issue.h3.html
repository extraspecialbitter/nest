Ed Markowski

lake trout–
a raven's caw
in with the knife

Odd G. Aksnes

 

nightfall
the shape of his knees
in the hanging jeans

Pamela Miller Ness

 

hot night
magnolia blossoms
in the moonlight

Rebecca Lily

 

no rain
spiders web the holes
in a patio umbrella

LeRoy Gorman

 

hot sun
a canoe full of corn grains
losing gold

Rosa Clement

 
 
 

dusk on the estuary–
a school of bass break
the shallow tide

Matt Morden

 

writing to an old friend–
a firefly just outside
my window

Leatrice Lifshitz

 

night music
fireworks mingle
with the stars

Celia Crook

 
summer haze–
the forest canopy
full of holes
Cindy Zackowitz

 

a thin trail
to the spider's nest . . .
wet paint

Ed Markowski
