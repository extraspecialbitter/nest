sun low
chins rested
on the losing oars

Helen Buckingham

 

dusk —
sandpipers jab
the foam

Martin Gottlieb Cohen

 

outside the wake
cigarettes
dot the dark

Jennifer Gomoll Popolis

 

autumn chill
the cracks I don’t
step on

Brendan Slater

 

autumn light
the cantor sings
a sinner’s prayer

David Caruso

 
 
 

almost a moon
the weight of the glass
as it empties

Francine Banwarth

 

my sister’s side
of our childhood
moonlit night

Michele Root-Bernstein

 

early autumn
the old priest
trembling with tolls

Andrea Cecon

 

blue asters —
God grant me
the serenity . . .

Christopher Patchel

 

shared grief
the splash of oars
in the fog

Jay Haskins
