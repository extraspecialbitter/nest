William Cullen Jr.

Gary Hotham

Terry Ann Carter

Andrea Grillo

Vaughan Chapman

Cherie Hunter Day

Matthew Paul

Grant D. Savage

Zinovy Vayman

LeRoy Gorman

harvest moon
over an empty wheat field
Gettysburg

William Cullen Jr.

 

just can’t help it
correcting the grammar
of the blues singer

Terry Ann Carter

 

full moon
he asks if I’m ready
to sing

Vaughan Chapman

 

dusky nettlebed
the stream just deep enough
for ducks to dabble in

Matthew Paul

 

falling oak leaf —
dance macabre
of its shadows

Zinovy Vayman

 
 
 

big city morning —
shadows hiding
my shadow

Gary Hotham

 

autumn clouds
a Carly Simon poster
fades into barnwood

Andrea Grillo

 

honking geese
the jumble of pumpkins
at a roadside stand

Cherie Hunter Day

 

yellow leaves in the current
a mink
after a mink

Grant D. Savage

 

no way out
Death’s at the door
demanding candy

LeRoy Gorman
