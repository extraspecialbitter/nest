Harriot West

Christopher Patchel

David Caruso

og aksnes

Duro Jaiye

jennie townsend

Robert Bauer

Jim Kacian

Tom Clausen

New Year’s Day
waiting
for the wishbone to dry

Harriot West

 

snowflakes . . .
no two winters
quite the same

David Caruso

 

the time it takes
to thaw the breast milk —
winter night

Duro Jaiye

 

winter seclusion
yeast froth rises
in candlelight

Robert Bauer

 

in the quiet
of a love scene
our cat purrs

Tom Clausen

 
 
 

decrystalizing
the jar of honey . . .
January sunrise

Christopher Patchel

 

a howl
crossing the ice
the moon

og aksnes

 

late in the day —
the hiss of water
boiling over

jennie townsend

 

winter thaw
the spilled seed cool
to the touch

Jim Kacian
