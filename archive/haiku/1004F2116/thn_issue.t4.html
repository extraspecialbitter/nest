the mountain pine —
      holding the light,
            held by it

Lorin Ford

 

“Bill’s Day” —
draw the letters
in aspen-glow

Karma Tenzing Wangchuk

 

a little cold
and without the bright fish
this golden pond

Bruce Ross

 

gray October
the poet’s voice
outlives him

David Lanoue

 

full moon
and so my thoughts
turn to you

Roberta Beary

 
 
 

vest pocket park
the leaves a little
less brilliant

Scott Mason

 

at his passing —
a ripple in the river
of stars

Karen Cesar

 

misty shroud —
leaves falling
between the rails

Claudia Brefeld

 

sunset fades
from the highest peak —
autumn chill

Laryalee Fraser

 

Higginson’s haiku
still among the living;
the autumn wind

Johnny Baranski
