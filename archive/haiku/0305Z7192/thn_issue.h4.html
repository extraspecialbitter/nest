Cherie Hunter Day

Lenard D. Moore

winter solstice
out-of-town snow
comes in with the coal train

Cindy Tebo

 

icy morning
a small crackling
from the woodstove

Alice Frampton

 

tabletop fountain–
the doctor's bedside manner
in his voice

Yvonne Cabalona

 

foot-long icicles–
what to do with the spider
in the fruit  bowl?

Kirsty Karkow

 

New Year's Day–
my son lets the tide
seep into his sneakers

Cherie Hunter Day

 
 
 

window frost–
the moon
through a thumbprint

Martin Vest

 

her hospital room–
snow filling the small field
next to the big one

Gary Hotham

 

Christmas Eve–
in the courtyard below
a flutter of wings

Pamela Miller Ness

 
whitecaps
across the inlet–
his salty beard
Peggy Lyles

 

new century–
the neighbor's rusty car
still on blocks

Lenard D. Moore
