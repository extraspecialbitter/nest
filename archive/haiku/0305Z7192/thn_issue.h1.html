William Cullen Jr.

Larry Kimmel

Florence Vilén

Zinovy Vayman

H. F. Noyes

Billie Wilson

Michael McClintock

Carolyn Hall

Elizabeth St Jacques

day moon
dandelion seeds
drift by

William Cullen Jr.

 

green tea in white cups
    an oriole holds forth
         from a high branch

Larry Kimmel

 

my synagogue route–
from a draped Moslem woman
Grandma's scent

Zinovy Vayman

 

sunny afternoon–
a bee follows wildflowers
into my car

Billie Wilson

 

empty sandals
on the beach
pull of the moon

Carolyn Hall

 
 
 

spring sky–
the unfocused stare
of a newborn

Kathy Lippard Cobb

 

dead pear tree–
for ten days each spring
it was white with blossom

Florence Vilén

 

a few snow patches–
white steer faces peer
above wildflowers

H. F. Noyes

 
a black bear
sunning
daisy-strewn
Michael McClintock

 

a Cajun song
in the evening air
lilacscent

Elizabeth St Jacques
