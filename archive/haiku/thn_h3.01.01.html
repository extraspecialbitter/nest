Jane Reichhold

Francine Porad

John Stevenson

Garry Gay

Ferris Gilli

Elizabeth St Jacques

Carol O'Dell

Christopher Herold

H. F. Noyes

Kay F. Anderson

mist
over the mountain pass
a bridge
Jane Reichhold

 

falling leaves—
several trees
in the woodpile
John Stevenson

 

wooden bridge
a nuthatch creeps
toward its reflection
Ferris Gilli

 

blues on the bay
how heavy it's become
this December sky
Carol O'Dell

 

frost-sealed woods—
in the thick of black cloud
the moon finds a rift
H. F. Noyes

 
 
 

migrating birds
grains of sand tumble
from her notebook
Francine Porad

 

At the top
of the totem pole
real crow
Garry Gay

 

clearing
the barbed wire fence
a wounded doe
Elizabeth St Jacques

 

sudden sun
     hailstones begin to pool
     in the birdbath
Christopher Herold

 

winter moonlight
seeping through . . .
      owl calls
Kay F. Anderson
