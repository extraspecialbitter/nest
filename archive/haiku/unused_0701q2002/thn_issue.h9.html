late November . . .
I miss another visit
to the nursing home

Charles Trumbull

 

slender moon —
locking the door
for the last time

Timothy Hawkes

 

Pearl Harbor Day
the back yard fence steams
in the morning sun

Lane Parker

 

every one
of her Xmas cookies
the same snowflake

Scott Metz

 

dirt road —
ahead and behind
falling leaves

Rick Tarquinio

 
 
 

autumn elms —
under her arm
a folded star

ron moss

 

moonlight —
reducing the city
to ruins

Sean MacMathuna

 

Christmas Eve —
on the hairdresser’s face
a hint of tears

Marili Deandrea

 

bowing
one last time —
frozen grass

Helga Härle

 

sickle moon
an exit ramp bends
through black ice

Scott Mason
