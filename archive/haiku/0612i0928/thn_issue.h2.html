year’s end
the bartender
blocks my reflection

Tom Painting

 

winter day —
too tired to make the clouds
anything but clouds

Lane Parker

 

first blossoms
a breeze shifts something
in each room

Burnell Lippy

 

robins outside—
the piano tuner
works on middle C

Charlie Close

 

cycling uphill —
the butterfly
passes me

Jason Sanford Brown

 
 
 

chemotherapy
her children’s names
tattooed on her shoulder

Victor Ortiz

 

doe and yearling
fade into the woods
hepatica in bloom

Grant D. Savage

 

spring morning
the children’s game
of being quiet

Marcus Larsson

 

sunny morning —
his basket full
of spinning pinwheels

K. Ramesh

 

cherry blossom hill . . .
children coming down
where there is no path

Scott Metz
