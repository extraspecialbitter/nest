Johnnie Johnson Hafernik

Joyce Clement

Helen Buckingham

Urszula Wielanowska

rustle of palm leaves . . .
fishermen play cards
in the boat’s shade

K. Ramesh

 

roadside walk
a beggar stretches
leg as hand

Adjei Agyei-Baah

 

fiddlers’ festival
the bull frogs’
uneven harmony

Peg McAulay Byrd

 

alone
with Dad’s old songs
another night of fireflies

Joyce Clement

 

    adonis blue . . .
  searching in vain
among the heathcliffs

Helen Buckingham

 
 
 

summer afternoon —
the saxophone busker
takes a drag

Marshall Bood

 

tornado watch
cricket songs carried
from tall grass

Johnnie Johnson Hafernik

 

Gypsy violin . . .
the wheat harvest
sparkling with fireflies

Tatjana Debeljački

 

mountain pass wind
whistles through my pack frame —
a pika answers

Ruth Yarrow

 

crescent moon
quiet lullaby
from the rocker

Urszula Wielanowska
