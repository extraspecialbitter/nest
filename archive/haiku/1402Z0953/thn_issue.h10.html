my housefly’s autumn
the window with the hills
of apple trees

Michael McClintock

 

moonlit waves
the glow of streetlights
on clapboard cottages

Garry Eaton

 

snow in the forecast —
some extra spice
in the Jambalya

Paul David Mena

 

12.01 am
flying a drunkard’s path
the year’s first moth

Violette Rose-Jones

 

snow erasing
the garden’s features —
teaching my dad to shave

Mathew Spano

 
 
 

unilingual
I study the expressions
in a wren’s eye

Merrill Ann Gonzales

 

first cold day
the cat kneads
a patch of sunlight

Lauren Mayhew

 

first flakes
discovering my childhood
in a box

Dietmar Tauchner

 

Orion’s belt
my words byte by byte
to another continent

Deb Baker

 

after the funeral
snow covers
the dark places
