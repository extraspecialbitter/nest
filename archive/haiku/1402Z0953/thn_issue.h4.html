piano practice
leaves swirl
allegro con brio

Roberta Beary

 

scudding moon
a starfish holds fast
to the hull

Lorin Ford

 

neck
& neck —
geese

Alan S. Bridges

 

first names
no longer in fashion
weathered headstones

John Soules

 

between roots and branches
centuries
and a child’s swing

Seánan Forbes

 
 
 

autumn wind
I take what I want
of the apples

Gregory Hopkins

 

October fest
too much oom-pah-pah
in the mustard

Adelaide Shaw

 

leaves in the wind . . .
this time
I let him go

Naia

 

godless month
a double rainbow
spans the mourners

Berenice Mortimer

 

first snow
cascading through cedar
a woodpecker’s call

Margaret Dornaus
