    • Please submit haiku for The Heron's Nest, a monthly web journal.

    We are accepting both traditional haiku (approximately 5-7-5 with a seasonal reference) and modern haiku.

    Unpublished poems should not be under consideration elsewhere. Previously published poems are fine, but please send publication details with your submission. Include your city, state, and country for our author index. Your e-mail will be listed with your name and city unless you request otherwise.

    For an upcoming issue, the deadline for submissions is the third Sunday of each month. A new edition of The Heron's Nest will be posted two Sundays after each submission deadline. Included will be several pages of haiku and one page presenting three "Editor's Choice" haiku. One of these three will receive The Heron's Nest Award and be posted on the home page. This haiku will receive special commentary. Each January, the Editor's Best-of-the-Year prize will be awarded to a haiku from the Editor's Choice selections of the previous year. Also in January, one haiku will receive the Readers' Choice prize. All haiku published in The Heron's Nest during the previous year are eligible. Voting will take place once the December issue has been posted and will conclude at midnight on New Year's Eve.

    Every issue of The Heron's Nest will be printed and a copy sent to the American Haiku Archives, a repository for modern English language haiku. A paper copy will also be sent to the Museum of Haiku Literature in Tokyo, Japan.

    Subscriptions to a paper edition of The Heron's Nest can obtained by contacting Christopher Herold. A year's subscription (twelve issues plus the year-end awards issue) can be obtained for US$15 in the United States; US$17 in Canada; US$20 elsewhere. Single paper copies cost US$1.25 each in the United States; US$1.50 each in Canada: US$1.75 each elsewhere. Please make checks or money orders payable in US funds to Christopher Herold (not The Heron's Nest), and send to his home address.
