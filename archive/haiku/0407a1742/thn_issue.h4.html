D. Claire Gallagher

 

first light–
guessing at the depth
of overnight snow

Cindy Zackowitz

 

a little girl
watching a cocoon . . .
wings

Fran Masat

 

clear lake
spring clouds glide
towards the dock

Carmen Sterba

 

cherry blossoms
the parish priest
straightens his collar

paul m.

 
 
 

windblown rain
the labored purring
of an old cat

Kirsty Karkow

 

an osprey's nest
even here
the use of plastic

Joann Klontz

 

budding trees–
chafe marks
from new sandals

Maria Steyn

 

rural prison–
larksong drawing up faces
from the pens

Sean MacMathuna

 

cold rain
cherry petals in the street
with lotto tickets

William Cullen, Jr.
