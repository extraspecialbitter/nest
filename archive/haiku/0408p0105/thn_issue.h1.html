curve by curve
I follow her down
the river

Jim Kacian

 

summer solstice–
a school of minnows suspended
in the stream

Christopher Patchel

 

forest path
a spider web
catches my ear

Doris Thurston

 

eyes closed
to a new morning–
mockingbird melodies

Kay F. Anderson

 

not watching the fireworks
          the big black dog
              in a child's lap

Sabine Miller

 
 
 

a cool current
where the river deepens
summer sky

Peggy Lyles

 

flattened grass
where the bear slept
stink of salmon

Billie Wilson

 

silent Friends meeting . . .
the sound of chairs being moved
to enlarge the circle

Robert Major

 

dewpoint . . .
the kleenex flowers wilt
on a parade float

an'ya

 

Taos sunrise–
a polished pot
on the adobe sill

Ann K. Schwader
