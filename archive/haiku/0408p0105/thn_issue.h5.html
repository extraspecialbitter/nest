last night's rain
the bird bath full
of sparrows

Angelee Deodhar

 

new leaves–
the tree growing
through a roof's remains

Fran Masat

 

a boy pitching pennies
into the water basin–
lowering clouds

Lenard D. Moore

 

summer humidity:
           the circular motion
                      of the carousel

Shirley Cahayom

 

between innings–
the joy of the game
on my son's face

Curtis Dunlap

 
 
 

clearing sky
a child washes pebbles
in a puddle

Kirsty Karkow

 

ready to garden–
in the potting shed she gathers
her love handles

Evelyn Hermann

 

tornado sky
the mockingbird keeps
right on singing

Rick Tarquinio

 

midday train ride
fanning the child
with Dr. Seuss

Raoul Fernandes

 

summer evening
the woodpecker
finishes

Gary Steinberg
