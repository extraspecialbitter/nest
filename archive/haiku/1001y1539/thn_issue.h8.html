my bare feet
among heron tracks
ebb tide

Lynn Edge

 

a smear of stars
from horizon to horizon —
the loon’s necklace

D. Claire Gallagher

 

summer house —
the key sticks
when leaving

Adelaide B. Shaw

 

nameless longings
a floating seed
eludes my hand

Christopher Patchel

 

vacant playground
the shadow of the swing
wanders off

C. Avery

 
 
 

thin moon
a harrier hawk
working the field

paul m.

 

end of summer
the sheep the color
of parched grass

Joann Klontz

 

first cool night . . .
I dream of knitting
firefly sweaters

Nancy Stewart Smith

 

tobacco harvest
women in lamplight
tie the quilt

L. Teresa Church

 

rural school . . .
the shy smiles of children
waiting for the bus

K. Ramesh
