cottonwoods
over a slow stream
the heat

Ann Schwader

 

his 44th —
water that doesn’t run back
into the ocean

Gary Hotham

 

more war dead —
an uprooted tree
rides the storm tide

Billie Wilson

 

gray afternoon
a ladybug escapes
the storm window

Deb Baker

 

Vivaldi . . .
olive leaves ripple
silver

Giselle Maya

 
 
 

summer sky
my father counts the black faces
on my road

Glenn G. Coats

 

Venus rising
a coyote trap
snaps shut

Lynne Steel

 

in the season
of wandering dragonflies
my urge to roam

Margarita Engle

 

storm clouds —
hearing the pauses
in the katydid’s song

Hilary Tann

 

night of fireflies —
fishing lights
off the island

Jian Zheng
