scent
of raked leaves —
an old wound

Giselle Maya

 

autumn sun
the man on a bicycle
hugs his pumpkin

Robert Witmer

 

autumn twilight
I tell the hostel
we’re almost there

Marcus Larsson

 

black fingers
in the dough
a hint of winter

Lenard Moore

 

a faint chance of snow
“the right money moves
for the year ahead”

Merrill Ann Gonzales

 
 
 

autumn woods
my son and I
not just DNA

Patricia Klein

 

Halloween
the white cat’s
black shadow

Gregory Hopkins

 

the sky
gray with voices
late geese

Ann K. Schwader

 

shorter days
my Medicare card
arrives in the mail

Joann Klontz

 

emptying the trap . . .
a pumpkin seed between
the mouse’s teeth

Nathalie Buckland
