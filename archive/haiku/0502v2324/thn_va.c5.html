Special Mentions — Page 1


Several poems scored far above the average, and a few poets accumulated high overall point scores for their respective bodies of work. It is clear that these poets and poems have made deep and lasting impressions and richly deserve to be honored by inclusion in this Valentine Awards issue.

religion aside
there are plum blossoms
and pussywillows

H. F. Noyes

 
nine-month belly—
she slowly unwraps
the heirloom crèche
D. Claire Gallagher 
 
7th floor, cubicle 9—
a butterfly drifts
past the window 
Timothy Hawkes 
 
studio window—
a fly walks
from cloud to cloud 
Kuniharu Shimizu 
 
Tiananmen Square
I buy a kite
shaped like a bird 
Linda Robeck 
 
empty tracks
a stranger and I
looking in the same direction 
Yu Chang 
 
winter landscape
one more raven flaps out
of her paintbrush 
Marian Olson 
 
spring mist—
a mallard paddles
through our stillborn’s ashes 
H. Gene Murtha 
 
spring twilight—
the unexpected weight
of a swept snail shell 
Yvonne Cabalona
