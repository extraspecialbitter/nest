gooseflesh
in the cold grass
crickets

Peter Yovu

 

her death bed
the clackety-clack-clack
of my homebound train

Carolyn Hall

 

he tells me the word
I’m looking for
total eclipse

Helen Russell

 

late autumn
a doll that lost
its sawdust

Dru Philippou

 

guttering candle . . .
a whole life lived without
the Northern Lights

Kirsty Karkow

 
 
 

falling leaves
   the knot that makes
      two lines one

James Chessing

 

wetting fingers
to pick up crumbs
insomnia

Richard Straw

 

a carpet of leaves . . .
I try to remember
her first smile

John Kinory

 

fallow field
the frosted shadow
of a scarecrow

André Surridge

 

Christmas Eve —
candles flicker through
our wine glasses

Angelee Deodhar
