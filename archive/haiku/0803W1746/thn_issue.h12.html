grease oozes
from a Ferris wheel
the long day

Robert Bauer

 

farmers’ market —
fruitflies pointing out
the ripe ones

Karma Tenzing Wangchuk

 

the coolness
of evening grass . . .
our first time

Keith Jennings

 

sunny morning —
the rooster challenges
the bull again

K. Ramesh

 

back at work —
the mosquito bite
blossoms

Roberta Beary

 
 
 

midsummer —
the uneven whirl
of a hula hoop

Robert Bauer

 

girl fiddler last to play
      sugar at the bottom
               of the lemonade cup

William Palmer

 

biting into the fig
how red
his heart tattoo

Sandra Simpson

 

power outage —
forced outside to read
I put down my book

Andrea Grillo

 

city sidewalk
colors swirl in a bubble
of spit

Brenda J. Gannam
