glowing coals
buried in the ashes —
peepers at dawn

Jack Barry

 

first light —
not enough words
for green

Susan Constable

 

dawn yoga —
forms change
with a rooster’s call

Francis Masat

 

funeral
her long hair follows
the wind

Marili Deandrea

 

turning the corner —
a breath of laundry
and lilacs

Ann K. Schwader

 
 
 

moonlight
on the sapling’s branch
an unexpected flower

Valeria Cecon

 

pulled from sleep
by the solstice moon . . .
cowbells

Kate MacQueen

 

Ascension Day . . .
pastry crumbs
for the bird-table

Helen Buckingham

 

cathedral shade —
a bee deep inside
the bearded iris

Matthew Paul

 

the laughter
of elderly friends
magnolia rain

D. Claire Gallagher
