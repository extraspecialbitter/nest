A Clump of Scum

Rengay written July 28, 1999 in the serenity chat room.

  • Christopher Herold
  • Paul MacNiel
Copyright © 1999. All rights reserved by the respective authors. 

a moose lifts its head
the clump of scum in its mouth
drips into the pond
                           Christopher

the red convertible drives
under the waterfall
                           Paul

Christmas eve--
a pine needle falls into
the tiny boxcar
                           Christopher

a pilgrim sinks
to his knees
the polished floor
                           Paul

again her bowling ball
rolls into a gutter
                           Christopher

autumn evening
a cigarette butt´s
last coil of smoke
                           Paul
