Along the Beach

Renku begun Saturday, September 11, 1999.

  • Alex Benedict
  • Alice Benedict
  • Christopher Herold
  • Carol O'Dell 
    • 


1   autumn
      along the beach
      we talk of the days
      like falling leaves
                                Alex

2   autumn
      broken up in wavelets
      reflection of the huge moon
                                Alice

3   autumn
      a drop from the hose
      coagulates the dust
      on a persimmon
                                Christopher

4   no kigo
      school playground
      two friends make mud pies
                                Carol

5   summer
      mosquitoes
      I watch them land on you
      and take off again
                                Christopher

6   summer
      her window left open
      for the warm breeze
                                Alex

7   no kigo
     the lost suitcase
     on top of a loaded cart
     in the hotel lobby
                                Alice

8   love
      meeting for the last time
      he tells of his wife and child
                                Carol

9   love
      bedside vigil
      something in the way
      the nurse fidgets
                                Christopher

10   love
        petal by petal
       "he loves me, he loves me not"
                                Carol

11   no kigo
         new age seance
         dabs of essential oil
         on the candle wicks
                                Alice

12   no kigo
         the flywheel spins
         the potter's hands
                                Alex

13   winter
         cold moon
         the deep black fissures
         in half dome
                                Alice

14   winter
         snow-covered field
         impressions of angels
                                Carol

15   no kigo
         your birthday cake iced
         a telltale smear of chocolate
         on my collar
                                Christopher

16   no kigo
         wrapped in silk
         her favorite jewels
                                Alex

17   cherry blossoms
         beyond the barrack walls
         blossoming cherry trees, eerie
         under floodlights
                                Christopher

18   spring
         finally, another duckling
         out of the nest box
                                Alice

19   spring
         the kite soars
         higher and higher--
         to the end of the string
                                Carol

20   no kigo
         space walk
         the only sound, each breath
                                Christopher

21   no kigo
         crowned in thorns
         the mendicant trails
         behind
                                Alex

22   no kigo
         zapped again!
         the corral's electric fence
                                Christopher

23   summer
         the dome and spires
         of the Taj Mahal still white
         in this heat haze
                                Alice

24   summer
         he licks drips
         from the ice cream cone
                                Carol

25   no kigo
         unable to deal
         the ace from the bottom...
         sticky cards
                                Christopher

26   love
         scattered here and there
         all their clothes
                                Alex

27   love
         lovers for thirty years
         he knows her body
         as well as his own
                                Carol
28   no season

29   moon

30   autumn

31   autumn

32   no season

33   no season

34   no season

35   cherry blossom

36   spring
