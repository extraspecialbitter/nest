Tail Thumps

Rengay with a sound topic written July 21, 1999 in the serenity chat room.

  • Ferris Gilli
  • Alex Benedict
Copyright © 1999. All rights reserved by the respective authors. 

in rhythm
with Grandpa's rocker
the hound's tail thumps
                            Ferris

picking up one-two one-two
tap improvisation
                            Alex

just before dawn
a woodpecker claims
the roof gutter                            
                            Ferris
                            
swallowing loudly
he looks around "I guess
its my turn next"
                            Alex
                            
she waits for the upstairs guy
to drop the other shoe
                            Ferris
                            
we laugh
today not even the biggest one
          got away
                            Alex
