Another Gull

Rengay written July 28, 1999 in the serenity chat room.

  • Paul MacNiel
  • Christopher Herold
Copyright © 1999. All rights reserved by the respective authors. 

spruce headland
another gull
wheels left
                         Paul

just before the thunderheads
a stiff wind
                         Christopher

jello
on the picnic table
shaking
                         Paul

she smiles seductively
and runs her toe
up my thigh
                         Christopher

half way through the bean supper
a surprise
                         Paul

early morning sun
this park bench was not
part of my dream
                         Christopher
