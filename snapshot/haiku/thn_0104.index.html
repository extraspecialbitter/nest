    • Please submit haiku for The Heron's Nest, a monthly web journal.

    We are accepting both traditional (approximately 5-7-5 with a seasonal reference) and modern haiku.

    Unpublished poems should not be under consideration elsewhere. Previously published poems are okay; send publication details with your submission. Please include your city, state, and country for our author index. Your e-mail will be listed with your name and city unless you request otherwise.

    A new edition of The Heron's Nest will be posted during the first week of each month. Included will be three pages of haiku and one page presenting three "Editor's Choice" haiku. One of these three will receive The Heron's Nest Award and be posted on the home page. This haiku will receive special commentary. Each January, a Best of the Year Prize will be awarded to a poem from the Editor's Choice selections.

    Every issue of The Heron's Nest will be printed and a copy sent to the American Haiku Archives, a repository for modern English language haiku. A paper copy will be sent to poets included in the issue. If you would like a paper copy of The Heron's Nest please contact Christopher Herold.

    You can email your haiku to Christopher Herold. Submissions will also be accepted via regular mail (please include SASE) to:
