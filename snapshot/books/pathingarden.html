A Path in the Garden

haiku by

Christopher Herold

Editor of The Heron's Nest

SAMPLE HAIKU:

dragonfly . . .
a stream of thought pauses
on the stone buddha

 

a warm gust . . .
back through the gate it comes
the whole pile of leaves

 

hot night
within the din of crickets
a single cricket
