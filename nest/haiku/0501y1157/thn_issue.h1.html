Connie Donleycott

 

horned owl
near the window
a curtain inhales

Elizabeth Howard

 

first morning –
dunking the last
of the homemade bread

Emily Romano

 

overcast sky –
adding more driftwood
to the bird blind

Cherie Hunter Day

river stones
worn smooth

Timothy Hawkes

the final hour

Marian Olson

 

wee hours –
the leaves on the roof
shift again

Barry George

a red fox drinks
from the swimming pool

Lynne Steel

 

winter morning
a ring of cattails
holds the mist

Jennie Townsend

 

winter clouds
a neighbor's résumé
left in our mailbox

Joann Klontz
