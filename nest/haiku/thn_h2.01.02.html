Jane Reichhold

 

Blue morning light
at the writing desk–
the rain falls harder

Del Doughty

 

where nobody cares,
growing with green vigor
the sumac

Paul O. Williams

 

one limb at a time–
the falcon calls her fledglings
nearer to flight

an´ya

 

knocked off by rain
camellias bloom again
in the puddles

Jane Reichhold

the winter

an´ya

again the playground fills
with pigeons

Charles Trumbull

the greening valley

wobbly legs

into a purr
