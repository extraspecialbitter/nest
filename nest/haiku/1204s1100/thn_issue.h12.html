new coolness
the old dog stays
closer to home

Barbara A. Taylor

 

half moon
your half interest
in the tryst

Robert Henry Poulin

 

seventh-inning stretch
a foam finger pointing
at the autumn moon

Mathew Spano

 

falling leaves
my friend and I
discuss clutter

Connie Donleycott

 

gray morning
the concrete business
of pigeons

Ann K. Schwader



 
 
 

rocking chairs
chained to the porch
leaves in the wind

Jack Barry

 

moon viewing —
one of the garden lanterns
lights up

Ruth Holzer

 

autumn wind
the leaves too
made of oak

Joyce Clement

 

All Hallow's Eve
moss-draped hemlocks
fade into night

Billie Wilson

 

the spiral
in a red hothouse rose —
year's end

Brent Partridge
