longest day
dipping cornbread
in buttermilk

Thomas Martin

 

heat wave
a fly moves slowly
away from my swat

Deb Baker

 

brush fire —
the sun goes down
in flames

Hortensia Anderson

 

withering heat
on the shed's tin roof
green black walnuts

Barbara Snow

 

last blackberries
again she playfully
tells me to shush

Matthew Paul

 
 
 

summer rain
waking to find the tent the size
of a mosquito

Gary Eaton

 

random beach stone
         imagining
       my biography

Scott Mason

 

field of sunflowers —
at the edge a boy
in a yellow hat

Ida Jones

 

summer's end
the wisteria
doubles back

Hilary Tann

 

moonrise —
     black swans make way
         for the rower

Lorin Ford
