A killing frost —
everything i couldn’t write
about chrysanthemums

vincent tripi

 

out of work —
the silverberry hedge
in a growth spurt

Cherie Hunter Day

 

cracked sidewalk
a young maple
branches out

Laryalee Fraser

 

fall breeze
a wasp struggles
with the screen door

Darrell Byrd

 

jays at the feeder
       I remember the names
       of high school bullies

Christopher Herold

 
 
 

fallen apple —
the bruise from the earth
cut off

Gary Hotham

 

deep autumn
a fish slides down
the cormorant’s throat

Ferris Gilli

 

autumn rain
settled
in my ways

John Stevenson

 

a path opens
through the pigeons —
wet maple leaves

William Cullen, Jr.

 

all its leaves fallen —
a tree we were
forbidden to climb

paul m.
