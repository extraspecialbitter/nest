     cats sunning
I speak their names
  their tails twitch

Robert Gibson

 

transient asleep
at the fountain's edge–

Martin Vest

 

a long line
at the drinking fountain
ants

Cindy Tebo

 

clouds billow
the island bridge swings open
for one ship

Nina A. Wicker

 

sultry day
a lizard rides my finger

the criss-cross of gull tracks
in damp sand

record heat–
the jade plant slumps over
a few yards from the hose
Carolyne Rohrig

 

swamp sunset–
the dragonfly rises
from a lily pad
