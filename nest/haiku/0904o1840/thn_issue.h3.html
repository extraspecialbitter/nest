New Year’s Eve —
rubbing velvet
the wrong way

Megan Arkenberg

 

winter moon
a smudged fingerprint
on the inkstone

Lorin Ford

 

white dawn . . .
the calf’s breath
on the udder

Ron Moss

 

magpie nestlings —
redgum widow-makers
creak in the wind

Lorin Ford

 

gray skies —
a kestrel
cries its kill

Ann K. Schwader

 
 
 

fireside
a piece of jigsaw
slips into place

Quendryth Young

 

waking beside you
the room glows
with snowlight

Ruth Yarrow

 

April sleet
oatmeal sticks
to the pot

Robbie Gamble

 

light rain —
the open beak
of the bird

Yoav J. Tenembaum

 

whistling back to them
I can’t find the right key. . .
tree frogs

Brenda J. Gannam
