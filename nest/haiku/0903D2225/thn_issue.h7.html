Raquel D. Bailey

spring training —
a father catches
his breath

Collin Barber

 

sun after rain
tadpoles in a circle
point inward

Brent Partridge

 

Good Friday
the sudden whiteness
of hawthorn

Katrina Shepherd

 

empty pan
on the radiator —
May begins

David Giacalone

 

holding high “mi”
on my air guitar —
spring morning

Vladislav Vassiliev

 
 
 

misty rain
the clown painting
he meant to leave me

Marilyn Murphy

 

soft greens
and cherries full bloom —
but peace?

Elizabeth Kusuda

 

Earth Day
only a cherry petal
in the donation jar

William Cullen, Jr.

 

the kite’s tail
ripped from knotty branches —
mockingbird song

Michael Dylan Welch

 

blue butterfly
a little girl whistling
to herself

Raquel D. Bailey
