spring morning
green lights all the way
to the freeway

Victor Ortiz

 

lost in apple blossoms
the man with dementia
knows where he’s going

LeRoy Gorman

 

hanging
over the backyard fence
twilight and wisteria

Tom Tico

 

loosening magnolias
I linger
in the darkness

Burnell Lippy

 

early rising
the door’s peek hole
streams sunshine

Yvonne Cabalona

 
 
 

choppy river —
bursts of the coxswain’s orders
on the wind

Barry George

 

Mother’s Day
filling the bare spots
with nasturtiums

Carolyn Hall

 

after the stars
trout bones
on a white plate

Elizabeth Nordeen

 

lilac scent
the sound of hair
through her brush

Grant Savage

 

freshly painted door —
a bumblebee explores
the color black

Adelaide B. Shaw
