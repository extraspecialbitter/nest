House for sale
a vine almost to the end
of the laundry line

George Swede

 

spring frost
the wind-up toy stopped
in midstride

Michele Root-Bernstein

 

source of the creek
a kinglet’s
breeding song

Allan Burns

 

early blossoms
the hummingbird’s throat
brighter still

Bruce Ross

 

two plovers
and their shadows
ploughed fields

Tony Beyer

 
 
 

sold sign
the nest
in the leafless tree

Sandra Mooney Ellerbeck

 

calving glacier
a mother whispers
to her child

Kay Grimnes

 

mountain torrent —
the water ouzel flicks
a white eyelid

Ruth Yarrow

 

the tips
of crabapple buds
April sunrise

Ann K. Schwader

 

lawn daisies
a boy tosses the ball
to himself

Harriot West
