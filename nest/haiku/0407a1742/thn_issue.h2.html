Gabriel Rosenstock

the occasional tapping
of a screen door

John W. Wisdom

the white eyes of a potato
beneath the cabinet

Lenard D. Moore

into the wind

in the makeshift shelter
an anthill

Dominique Chipot

the sound of rain
in the cornfield
