village road . . .
the jingle of bullock carts
in the mist

K. Ramesh

 

spring day
announcement said
no flowers

Ernest Wit

 

progress . . .
the cobweb repaired where
it crosses the gate latch

George Swede

 

dogwoods
the stench of
baby questions

Amelia Cotter

 

dancing
above the hedgerow
just her hat

David Ash

 
 
 

water
upon water
a spider into the bud

Kushal Poddar

 

Easter morning . . .
three silos
on the horizon

Melissa Allen

 

an eye
in the window
of a birdhouse

E. Jones

 

the pause
he takes on his flute —
spring rain

Kala Ramesh

 

age 88
all the whatchamacallits
in the spring wind

Joyce Clement
