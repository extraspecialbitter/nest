Alegria Imperial

post-op
the holy medal’s features
imprinted in my palm

Mathew V. Spano

 

hunter’s moon
the gator’s eyes
hold the light

William Cullen, Jr.

 

more space
for the raven’s voice
winter sky

Ann K. Schwader

 

cloud dragon
eating its own tail —
Epiphany Day

Brent Partridge

 

muted sunlight
the crisp corners
of the folded flag

Cara Holman

 
 
 

last light
prying a pigeon
from the dog’s mouth

Bob Lucky

 

frost-sparkled paddock
he digs the grave
a little deeper

Carolyn Hall

 

winter solstice
reading glasses
to see myself

Grant D. Savage

 

winter pear
my breath condenses
on the knife

J. Zimmerman

 

crocus buds —
secrets we’ve kept
from each other

Alegria Imperial
