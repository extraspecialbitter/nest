home for the weekend
a water lily’s root
trailing out of sight

paul m.

 

summer clouds
she talks about death
hypothetically

Collin Barber

 

fishing
where my brother stood —
twilight chill

H. Gene Murtha

 

cicada song
just enough embers
to rekindle the fire

Carolyn Hall

 

sale pending —
crunched apples scent
the sidewalk

Edith Bartholomeusz

 
 
 

moonflowers —
I weave a wreath
for my sister’s urn

Nara Bauer

 

mountain sunset
the mockingbird’s song
unanswered

Lynne Steel

 

fallen tree
on the river
I’ve yet to cross

Sabine Miller

 

sunrise —
goldenrod
in the first frost

Stephen Engleman

 

no rain for days —
a half-eaten apple taken away
from the fruit flies

Gary Hotham
