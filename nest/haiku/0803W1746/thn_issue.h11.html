Margarita Engle

morning rush hour —
an osprey points a fish
into the wind

Darrell Byrd

 

the still cumulus
a hum of voices
before the workshop

Gillena Cox

 

muggy night
our room fan ruffles
the front page

Connie Donleycott

 

bright morning —
walking the pain
from my heart

Hilary Tann

 

wandering
from birdsong to birdsong
city morning

Barry George

 
 
 

salt spray
a taste of peat
in my whisky

Quendryth Young

 

evening warmth —
latchkey kids play rummy
in the doorway

Rebecca Lilly

 

sunrise
we lower
the blinds

Yu Chang

 

crowded square —
a mime’s glance
crosses mine

Andrea Cecon

 

merry-go-round
no one wants to ride
the wolf

Margarita Engle
