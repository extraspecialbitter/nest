Chad Lee Robinson

heat lightning
places where the mower
cut too low

Burnell Lippy

 

Key West sunset —
trading one shell
for another

Paula Fisher

 

the faded blue
of a lifeguard station —
empty shells in my pocket

Cherie Hunter Day

 

a beach ball
the gentle slap
of the moon

og aksnes

 

where the boys sat
a semi-circle
of watermelon seeds

Emily Romano

 
 
 

thunderhead
a snake’s path
slicing up the dune

Kay Grimnes

 

warm rain
his childhood memories
about the beach

Joann Klontz

 

soft-shell crabs —
the toddler shows off
her belly button

Linda Jeannette Ward

 

weathered dock —
a child reaches out
to smooth a wave

Carmen Sterba

 

wind in the tall grass . . .
an old blue car
almost in motion

Chad Lee Robinson
