William Cullen, Jr.

Tom Tico

Alice Frampton

Shane Robitaille

winter morning —
dad’s butter in a cup
atop the toaster

Nancy Stewart Smith

 

winter morning
her perfume scents
the river path

frances angela

 

the last of winter
the crucifix covered
with a purple shroud

Tom Tico

 

March wind
the story of a couple
who separate

Jerry Ball

 

mating robins
the broken yellow
of the centerline

Alice Frampton

 
 
 

children’s laughter —
a weekend father
with sticks in his hair

ron moss

 

church bells
tree roots have cracked
the sidewalk

William Cullen, Jr.

 

clearing sky
a hailstone melts
on the fallen flower

Nathalie Buckland

 

the void she leaves . . .
a honeybee enters
an apple blossom

Dietmar Tauchner

 

first buds
she only counts
the green cars

Shane Robitaille
