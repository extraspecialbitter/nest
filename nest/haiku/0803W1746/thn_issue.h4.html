Thanksgiving
my brother and I catch up
at halftime

John S. O’Connor

 

open windows
I switch off a concerto
to hear the trees

Deb Baker

 

sweet tea steam
dissolving
the man in the moon

C. Avery

 

turning the page . . .
these photographs
mother never pasted in

John Kinory

 

empty clothesline —
the mockingbird
begins his song

George Dorsty

 
 
 

slant of light
through autumn woods —
whitethroat’s minor whistle

Ruth Yarrow

 

full moon —
the baby monitor
sighs

Vladislav Vassiliev

 

pine scent in the air —
a memory of Memère
cleaning house

Mike Montreuil

 

winter dawn
a tub of washing
all run blue

Matt Morden

 

frosty morning
steam rises
from the horses’ blankets

Patricia Prime
