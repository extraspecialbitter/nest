spring shower
dark hands skinning catfish
in the kitchen sink

Francis W. Alexander

 

leaf buds
the flycatcher
varies his call

Carolyn Hall

 

almost no
cards to buy
Father’s Day

Carlos Colon

 

dawn
the broken edge
of a blue shell

Michele Root-Bernstein

 

Sunday morning —
gathered ibis
drown out the prayer

Graham Nunn

 
 
 

rainy season . . .
a smile on everything
the child draws

Paul Pfleuger, Jr.

 

searching willow wands
for the purple phoebe
my water bottle flutes

Kay F. Anderson

 

his parting gift:
origami swans
lasting this long

Janelle Barrera

 

day moon
a girl with dog tags
skipping rope

Darrell Lindsey

 

gardenia
just knowing
its scent

Yvonne Cabalona
