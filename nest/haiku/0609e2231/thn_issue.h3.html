black pond
swallowing snowflakes

Jack Barry

 

ice melted —
she reads Garcia Marquez
under a cat

Shimi

 

spring wind
the sprinter dashes
the other way

Lenard D. Moore

 

The evening star . . .
      our flower-viewing faces
           begin to change

vincent tripi

 

twilit city
creek pebbles
in my shoe

Paul Pfleuger, Jr.

 
 
 

silent spring —
a Cooper’s hawk
stalking the bushes

Elizabeth Howard

 

bilingual children
     honey bees move
     into the bird house

Francine Porad

 

cloudless sky —
in Mayan nine words
for the color blue

Lynne Steel

 

steel band at twilight —
a single file of ducklings
crosses the bay

Barry George

 

night heat —
the rising scent
of mosquito repellant

Cindy Zackowitz
