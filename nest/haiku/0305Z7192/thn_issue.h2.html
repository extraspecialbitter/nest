rising moon . . .
a slug's path
on a zucchini leaf

Naomi Brown

 

        prairie flowers
gone to seed    the dry flick
                 of grasshoppers

Christopher Patchel

 

unseasonable heat–
a woodpecker
in the lightning scar

Cindy Zackowitz

 

mountain silence
a leaf floats in the gorge
where a boxcar rusts

Marianne Bluger

 

a locomotive whistle
fills the tea room
evening chill

Bruce Kennedy

 
 
 

summer stream
a toddler's leaf floats
in circles

Lori Laliberte-Carey

 

approaching storm–
minnows dart
in the bait bucket

Brian Gierat

 

twilight
the meadow
cricketing

Evelyn Hermann

 
fallen leaves
settle deeper–
the hammock's sag
Harold Bowes

 

hermitage
a small hole dug
deep in an acorn

Matt Morden
