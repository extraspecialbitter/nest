John W. Wisdom
Heron's Nest Award

Yu Chang

Cindy Zackowitz


 
 

the slow turn
of a barber's pole–
afternoon heat

John W. Wisdom

 
 

 
 

back at camp
the mountain peak
still in my legs

 
 
 

black water–
a goose paddles
through morning snow

Yu Chang

 

Cindy Zackowitz
