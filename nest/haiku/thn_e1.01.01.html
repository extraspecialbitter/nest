Patricia Machmiller

Paul MacNeil

 
 

the Milky Way streams —
a farmer bends to his work
widening a ditch

H. F. Noyes

 
 

 
 

intersecting lines —
in the far distance a train
becomes the spring sky

 
 
 

high moon
all the shadows gone
from the haystack

Patricia Machmiller
