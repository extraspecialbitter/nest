back and forth
leaf shadow weaves
over the loom

Jane Reichhold

 

grandmother
giving chase again
the fat turkey

Elizabeth St Jacques

 

deserted beach
    missing a child's
    hand to hold

Francine Porad

 

deep winter–
a man outside the market
feeds crows from his hand

Billie Wilson

 

ax and pine
with every chop
a higher sound

Zinovy Vayman

 
 
 

autumn gusts
the ragged flight path
of a grey heron

John Crook

 

bubblegum on my shoe
the little girl in the red coat
skips to school

Kathy Lippard Cobb

 

lightning storm
bridge cards face-down
on the table

Carolyne Rohrig

 
northern lights
these problems
suddenly small
Marjorie Buettner

 

winter rain
against the window
the cactus has flowered

Sue Mill
