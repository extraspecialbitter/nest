Connie Donleycott

the fallen leaves

Richard Krawiec

 

snow-covered hills —
my sister and I coast
into the New Year

hortensia anderson

across the wall
my long shadow

expanding universe
the homeless man looks
for a place to sleep

Dietmar Tauchner

to Mongolia . . .
winter stars
