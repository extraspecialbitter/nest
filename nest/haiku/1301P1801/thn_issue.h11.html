midnight moon
dark stalks of Spanish Dagger
pierce the snow

Lynn Edge

 

soft rain
the clatter of young barn owls
trying their wings

James Chessing

 

bleak morning
her footprints
filled with rain

Gavin Austin

 

first love
all but forgotten
the meadowlark’s song

Cynthia Cechota

 

sun clears the ridge
a finch’s gold
flickers in the woodpile

John Barlow

 
 
 

eggshell fragments
what I know of the mother
I’ve never known

Melissa Spurr

 

the cries
in the marsh rain
jacksnipes

Martin Gottlieb Cohen

 

old cemetery
the gate swings
both ways

Susan Constable

 

bathed in sunlight
the day roost
of the rufous owl

John Barlow

 

climbing out
of the ocean’s roar
cliffside daisy

paul m.
