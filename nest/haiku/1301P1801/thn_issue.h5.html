snap of a clipboard
in the all-day workshop
the heat

Lenard D. Moore

 

heat lightning —
Christmas beetles
spangle the fly screen

Lorin Ford

 

yet again
by the river
a pair of knickers

Owen Bullock

 

old pond —
if only my face
would stay so smooth

David Ash

 

bamboo bending
in the wind —
I bargain for mangos

L. Costa

 
 
 

know-it-alls talk
through rolled down windows
dusty road

Robert Huotari

 

village festival —
the last firework
joins the moon

Andrea Cecon

 

aftershock
a tremor lingers
in the dog

Melissa Spurr

 

waning summer moon
mom and I
compare hands

Yvonne Cabalona

 

summer’s end —
a dog-chewed stick
by the edge of the pond

Cindy Zackowitz
