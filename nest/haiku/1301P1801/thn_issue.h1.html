paul m.

spring morning
i decline the offer
of a draw

Jim Kacian

 

fountain pool
all the circles
where the coins used to be

Carlos Colón

 

no wind
no breath to lift the sails
baby swans

Irene Reed

 

the flag at half mast
orchard blossoms fill
the bigger picture

LeRoy Gorman

 

getting acquainted
some wild mint
in the garden

Stephen A. Peters

 
 
 

in a nut shell
there is never
enough room

Yu Chang

 

how to dress her
for eternity —
blossom rain

Carolyn Hall

 

on hold —
the rain
turning to snow

Ben Moeller-Gaa

 

hill climbing —
with each step beech leaves
younger and younger

Tomislav Maretic

 

bend in the river
swans introduce their young
to the flock

paul m.
