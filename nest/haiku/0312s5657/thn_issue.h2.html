mid-winter doldrums
the stone angel

Giovanni Malito

 

starlings leave
the leafless tree
the silence

Christopher Patchel

 

starless night–
a slice of stale bread
divided by five

J. B. Leong

 

l’heure bleu
I find my house
when the neighbor’s dog barks

Zinovy Vayman

 

melted snowman
one white cloud drifts
across the puddle

William Cullen, Jr.

 
 
 

ice moon . . .

to the salt lick

an’ya

 

twilight shadows
pruned from the honey locust–
cold wind

Ann K. Schwader

 

winter storm . . .
growing puddles
of candle wax

Connie Donleycott

 
winter morning
the rattle of a shuttle
across the white threads
Kay Grimnes

 

her gravestone
the smooth place
no longer there
