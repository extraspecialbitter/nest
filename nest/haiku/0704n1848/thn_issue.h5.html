spring downpour —
my little son teaches me
to fold paper boats

Natasha Levi

 

   sheep back
from shearing —
   spring breeze

Stephen Toft

 

mating antics
of a hummingbird
wind on the lake

paul m.

 

gala garden tour!
the overwhelming urge
to yank pokeberry

S. R. Spanyer

 

slow rain —
losing myself in
birdbath circles

Ann K. Schwader

 
 
 

sheets of rain
a calf huddles closer
to the cowherd

Angelee Deodhar

the dog tugs for
another scent

Tom Clausen

 

the sun
at its zenith
goldfinch song

John Barlow

 

garden trellis
a green snake loops
into our small talk

Marilyn Appl Walker

 

quiet evening
the wind has touched
a lilac bush

Bronislawa Sibiga
