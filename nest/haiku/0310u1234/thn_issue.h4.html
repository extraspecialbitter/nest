Cherie Hunter Day

Emily Romano

mid-argument–
a bumblebee
stumbles in clover

Matt Morden

 

a spider weaves
among the rosehips–
no one at home

Peggy Lyles

 

schoolyard quiet–
the scribble of ants
in a stone shadow

June Moreau

 

summer highway
puddles of light merge
with the sky

Hilary Tann

 

the striped racer
slithering to full length
canyon wildflowers

Cherie Hunter Day

 
 
 

passing clouds–
an ant swings
on a grass blade

Dragan J. Ristic

 

my daughter's graduation–
a gull separates
from the rest of the flock

Paul David Mena

 

the baby nods
cottonwood seeds
drift along the road

John Stevenson

 
a migrant worker
in the 'native plants' median
midday heat
Mark Brooks

 

goldenrod
    bending
        heatwaves
