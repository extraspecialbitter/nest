No words can adequately express the feelings aroused in us by the recent terrorist attack.

We can only hope that the monstrous nature of this tragedy will serve to unite people around the world in mutual determination to eliminate terrorism and to achieve peace on Earth.

This issue of The Heron's Nest is dedicated to the people whose lives were lost or devastated because of the terrorist attack on September 11, 2001.
