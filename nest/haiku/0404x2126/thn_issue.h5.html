blackened forest
a mushroom
gnawed white

Anna Tambour

 

a small bowl
of imported oranges
snow outside

paul m.

 

winter sky
we decide to take
the slower road

Jerry Ball

 

spring fog . . .
a shoulder of mountain
crooked with pines

Michael McClintock

 

alleluias

the church

Gillena Cox

between branches the night
fades into sky

Ann K. Schwader

 

snowbound–
the orange tip
of a traffic cone

Patrick Kelly

a crack in the ice
follows the riverbend

Cindy Zackowitz

 

in the music room
with each in-breath
hyacinths
Sabine Miller

 

religion aside
there are plum blossoms
and pussywillows

H. F. Noyes
