Yu Chang

 

fall leaves
he tells me
I’ve changed

Katherine Cudney

 

autumn sun
I follow the shadow
with the limp

Jerry Ball

 

approaching winter
the fragrance of herbs
under sweet straw

Hilary Tann

 

autumn mist
the honey locust

Ann K. Schwader

 
 
 

sweet grapes
the conversation passes
between friends

Hilary Tann

from the blacksmith’s coals
autumn sun

Lenard D. Moore

 

autumn’s scent
in the pile of leaves . . .
I take the dare

Connie Donleycott

 

bumper crop . . .
the pecan tree’s shadow
stippled in shells

Nancy Stewart Smith

 

late night —
a waitress repeats
the list of pies
