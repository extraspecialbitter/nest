Connie Donleycott

 

All Hallow's Eve
the summer people's porch light
on all day

Burnell Lippy

 

creeping fog
a little moonlight
crosses the river

Sonny Villafania

 

geese overhead
the dog stops licking
to listen

Joann Klontz

her perfume lingers
in the frosty air

J. B. Leong

Dan McCullough

the luminous whiskers
of a cat

Maria Steyn

in river mist–
year's end

Peter Macrow

in the headlights–
ghost stories
