Cherie Hunter Day

 

evening stillness
a bonfire log slips
into its own ash

Collin Barber

 

dawn light
on a red-tailed hawk
Indian summer

Victor Ortiz

 

old snag —
a woodpecker starts
the day

Alice Frampton

 

time change
most of the fallen leaves
hidden by others

Burnell Lippy

 
 
 

family reunion
the beach stones underfoot
more painful now

Robbie Gamble

 

around the table
three generations
and the dog

Tony Beyer

 

autumn sunrise
the bristled coat
of a Hereford steer

Quendryth Young

 

Indian summer
the road crew plays catch
with a hardhat

H. Gene Murtha

 

autumn light
the deer’s shadow
touches my toes

Susan Constable
