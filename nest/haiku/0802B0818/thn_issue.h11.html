this morning too
the light of the moon

Marian Olson

 

cloud stain
on the mountain
last year’s snow

Tony Beyer

 

New Year’s Day
the center of the chocolate
not what I expected

Carolyn Hall

 

blackout
    the sound
        of candles

Ross Vassilev

 

clear night —
snow shifts
on the windowpane

Hilary Tann

 
 
 

New Year’s Day —
deer tracks on both sides
of the fence

Edith Bartholomeusz

 

early winter —
a growing desire
to paint the ceiling

Jennie Townsend

 

winter isolation —
tossing one pair of socks
into the hamper

Ruth Holzer

 

snow moon —
our angels
somewhere underneath

Scott Metz

 

bitter wind
a man hunched over
a hole in the ice

og aksnes
