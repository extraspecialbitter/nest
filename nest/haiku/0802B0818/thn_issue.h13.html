more snow . . .
the brittleness
of the wishbone

Alice Frampton

 

country evening —
smoke crosses the river
far from the bridge

D. V. Rozic

 

empty platform —
my shadow grows longer
waiting for you

Jacek Margolak

 

deserted stadium
the caretaker kicks
a goal

Maria Steyn

 

first day of spring
a new bottle of ink
for an old fountain pen

Jerry Ball

 
 
 

the grinding gears
of a flatbed truck
snowflakes

Lenard D. Moore

 

train coming —
all the snowmen
begin to move

Helga Härle

 

winter rain . . .
the light
from the flower shop

Timothy Hawkes

 

vernal equinox —
winter overflows
the closet

Gary Steinberg

 

spring breeze
patches of old snow
in the junkyard

Patrick Kelly
