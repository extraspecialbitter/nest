PRAISE FOR THE PRINT EDITION
OF THE HERON’S NEST VOLUME VII

 

Dear Friends and Haiku Fans,

Fortunately, I had a good many more copies of Volume VII printed than were initially ordered. I still have a good supply too, but there’s a steady stream of orders coming in so, if you are thinking about buying a copy of the paper edition, don’t delay too long. And if you’re vacillating, read some of these enthusiastic responses we’ve been receiving from subscribers. They just might convince you that it would be nice to have a copy too.

With much gratitude,

Christopher Herold
