Naomi Brown

Hortensia Anderson

Lynne Leach

dark June night
     bulls' bellowing
     echoes across the hollow

Elizabeth Howard

 

ocean horizon–
touching the curvature
of a white shell

Jerry Kilbride

 

summer day–
walking barefoot beside
grandchildren's footprints

Billie Wilson

 

a flatbed
hauling scrap metal
summer heat

Barry George

 

sunlight on the wall–
brushing against the yellow
my deep blue shadow

Hortensia Anderson

 
 
 

   parking lot at dawn–
gulls roosting among
   upturned shopping carts

Jack Lent

 

morning sun
the peach's blush
deepens near the pit

Ferris Gilli

 

noon sun
picnic cheese
sweating

Michael Dylan Welch

 
a roadrunner stops
its tail still fanning
    midday heat
Naomi Brown

 

ripples of light
across an oak plank floor
the wheelchair's wobble

Lynne Leach
