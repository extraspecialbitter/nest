A new edition of The Heron's Nest is published during the first week of each month. Included are several pages of haiku and one page presenting three Editor's Choice haiku. In each issue The Heron's Nest Award is given to one haiku. The winning haiku is posted on the home page, receives special commentary, and a certificate is sent to the poet.
