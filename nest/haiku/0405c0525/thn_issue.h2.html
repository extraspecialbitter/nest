spring sky
a pair of ravens
owning it

Marian Olson

 

muddy barnyard
the old farmer waltzing
with a wayward cow

Elizabeth Howard

 

pruning old roses–
              the taste of blood
on my tongue

Katie Cameron

 

water strider . . .
the wake
of an oar

John S. O’Connor

 

creek crossing . . .
the uneven footing
on the other side

paul m.

 
 
 

breezy afternoon–
making a kite again
after many years

K. Ramesh

 

nature preserve–
encircling its marker
an endangered plant

Paul Watsky

 

a rest by the stream–
burrs on the tongue
of my tennis shoe

Michael Dylan Welch

 
mid-stream
a doe waits for her fawns
summer twilight
Carolyn Hall

 

a row of canoe paddles
hanging on a rail–
evening stillness

A. C. Missias
