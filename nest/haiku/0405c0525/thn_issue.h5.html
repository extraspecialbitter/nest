Darrell Byrd

 

spring sun
the whiteness
of my scar

William Cullen, Jr.

 

spring thaw
the splinter in my finger
finally comes out

MaryJane Turner

 

wild geese
nesting in the marsh grass . . .
she takes my hand

Michael Evans

 

honeymoon
we wade into the current
of a great river

Kirsty Karkow

 

 
 

by the statue
of the polar explorer
lingering snow

George Swede

the blind girl drinks
with cupped hands

Naomi Brown

where the tree was
spring runoff

LeRoy Gorman

 

lovers kissing
as the tulips close
spring rain
Paul Wigelius

 

butterflies
throughout the meadow–
gypsy music

an’ya
