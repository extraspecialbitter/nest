William Cullen, Jr.

sudden hope
as I leave the house —
magnolia

Dietmar Tauchner

 

Easter egg hunt
the smell of onion grass
on my son’s breath

Katherine Cudney

 

daffodil shoots —
all these years
as an accountant

paul m.

 

aged hands
a caterpillar
on her life line

Kirsty Karkow

 

dusk in the city
the parking attendant sings
with Pavarotti

Katherine Cudney

 
 
 

torn ceiling blocks —
near the finches’ nests
flutter and chatter

Francine Porad

 

new warmth —
the ruffled feathers
of the raven’s throat

Cindy Zackowitz

 

spring planting —
a redbird offers a seed
to his mate

Curtis Dunlap

 

first warm evening
Grandpa mimics
a swan

Robert Bauer

 

dandelion seed
drifting through the lawn party
her perfume

William Cullen, Jr.
