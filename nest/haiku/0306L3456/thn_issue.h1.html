Owen Burkhart

summer evening–
a boat returns to harbor
with pink sails

Billie Wilson

 

opened door
the darkness lengthens
into a kitten

Barry George

 

from the balcony above
a hand outstretched
. . . rain

Peter Williams

 

sunrise . . .
a mountain's shadow
touches the city

Salena Morris

 

fluttering leaves
the play of sunlight
on my eyelids

Stephen Amor

 
 
 

channel marker
fledgling ospreys
stretch their wings

Kirsty Karkow

 

handmade paper–
ink bleeding into
a late summer moon

Carolyn Thomas

 

reading by lamplight;
sounds of a summer night
press against the screen

Robert Major

 
summer heat
a cat drinks the water
in the birdbath
Carmel Lively

 

concert in the park–
sudden raindrops
change the tempo

Owen Burkhart
