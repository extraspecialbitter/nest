David Giacalone

William Cullen, Jr.

John Stevenson

Mitzi Trout

the cold
a stranger
at my parents’ grave

Jörgen Johansson

 

freezing rain
the yellow umbrella
of a hotdog stand

William Cullen, Jr.

 

a friend’s house —
shedding loneliness
with my coat

Mitzi Trout

 

 

 
 
 

alone —
hugging
warm laundry

David Giacalone

 

winter afternoon
millions of points
at pinball

John Stevenson
