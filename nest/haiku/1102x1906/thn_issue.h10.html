craggy overlook
a tail swinging
in the hawk’s beak

Elizabeth Howard

 

dry patches
on the schoolhouse lawn —
remedial math

Johns Elmwood

 

broken pavement . . .
she shows me
the blackberry patch

John Kinory

 

one turtle’s foot
on another’s back
labor day

Grant Savage

 

magpie season
the schoolgirl’s lunchbox
on her head

Cynthia Rowe

 
 
 

dull blade —
the way it opens up
a peach

Harriot West

 

after the funeral . . .
I rotate the chessboard
with each move

Jacek Margolak

 

Moving day
the children stick postage stamps
on the bare kitchen floor

Joan Zimmerman

 

harvest moon
a soup chicken thaws
in the sink

Jennifer Gomoll Popolis

 

fog lifting —
my cat finishes
a sparrow

Laurence Stacey
