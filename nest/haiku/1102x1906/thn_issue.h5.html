first love
the sterile flower
of Queen Anne’s Lace

Natalia L. Rudychev

 

slow, slow dusk
cicadas flood the pause
between apologies

Margaret D. McGee

 

caught
in the revolving door
                      sunset

Vladislav Vassiliev

 

ocean dusk
a tattler hauls a morsel
from the shallows

John Barlow

 

first cold night —
the plumber swears
in Polish

Cindy Zackowitz

 
 
 

windy afternoon
two beetles manage to mate
on the goldenrod

Jeffrey Rabkin

 

farm auction . . .
the green side
of a tomato

Connie Donleycott

 

the off season
leaves cover
the nude beach

LeRoy Gorman

 

a leaf
in no hurry
Indian summer

Ann K. Schwader

 

days shortening —
   his childhood sketches
   of dinosaurs

Lorin Ford
