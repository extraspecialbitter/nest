chess in the park . . .
an acorn
topples a king

Curtis Dunlap

 

sandcastles
new friends work on
old problems

Shane Robitaille

 

the child
blowing on a pinwheel —
the wind blowing on her

Terri L. French

 

cracked soil
a day laborer bent
over his shadow

Sasa Vazic

 

distant thunder
a revolutionary cannon
green with age

Yu Chang

 
 
 

summer night
a smoker’s cough and the clack
of mahjong tiles

Bob Lucky

 

sitting on the hill
of mom’s pregnant belly,
the Lego castle

Regina F. Smith

 

my kinfolk
don’t say a lot
hemlocks all around

Greg Piko

 

wildfire
vultures circling
the outer thermal

Don Miller

 

blue violets . . .
the coolness of evening
ascending the mountain

Michael McClintock
