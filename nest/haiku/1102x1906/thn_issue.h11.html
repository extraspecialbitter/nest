unemployment line
the autumn wind
fills with leaves

Collin Barber

 

solitude . . .
smoke from the incense
thickens a sunbeam

Christopher Herold

 

Father’s ashes
the sea spray
chills my face

Bett Angel-Stawarz

 

Christmas drizzle
my children come home
on different days

Lynn Edge

 

storm warning
the inflatable snowman
lying low . . .

Neil Moylan

 
 
 

lights from cities
a passenger’s shoulder
touches mine

Marcus Larsson

 

bedside vigil . . .
moonlight comes
and goes

Quendryth Young

 

cusp of winter —
passing into
the mountain’s shadow

Patrick M. Pilarski

 

minute of silence . . .
a single fir needle
on the hardwood floor

Tanya McDonald

 

winter fog
the minute hand
stuck in place

Michele Root-Bernstein
