Hilary Tann

 

winter moon . . .
fading echoes
of the coyote’s howl

Naomi Y. Brown

 

crows bicker
in the morning light —
our twisted sheets

George Dorsty

 

Chinese pork stall
the deep valley
in a chopping log

John Tiong Chunghoo

 

cloudy sky —
a little boy imitates
the cuckoo

K. Ramesh

 
 
 

jazz in the night
leaves blow across
the empty intersection

William Cullen, Jr.

 

the long night
a warm glow
from the baker’s window

Carolyn Hall

 

city morning
a crane lifts its shadow
up the wall

Jim Kacian

 

farmer’s market
the bee balm lady
drawing all the butterflies

Elizabeth Howard

 

sun shower . . .
the surveillance camera
fixed on a puddle

Paul Pfleuger, Jr.
