Cherie Hunter Day

William J. Higginson

Kay F. Anderson

spring joy
a twig dropped from the bridge
floats to the other side
Jane Reichhold

 

my son noticing . . .
the attention i pay
to butterflies
John Stevenson

 

beaded rain
in the crotch of wild cherry
tent caterpillars
Cherie Hunter Day

 

summer raindrops
she washes her feet
in the grass
Ferris Gilli

 

to cross the street
he hip-hops the heat waves
suburban crow
Kay F. Anderson

 
 
 

soft breeze–
a pair of butterflies alight
on a stalk of grass
Alex Benedict

 

in a flash
from sitting to hovering
the hummingbird
Paul O. Williams

 

Summer Again

summer dawn—
light around the windows
as at grandmother's

early morning
glue sets in the shade
of the old shed
William J. Higginson

 

gray morning
a spider drags its catch
along the eaves
Joann Klontz
