my housefly's autumn
the window with the hills
of apple trees

Michael McClintock
Clovis, California

 

moonlit waves
the glow of streetlights
on clapboard cottages

Garry Eaton
Port Moody, British Columbia, Canada

 

snow in the forecast—
some extra spice
in the Jambalya

Paul David Mena
Cochituate, Massachusetts

 

12:01 am
flying a drunkard's path
the year's first moth

Violette Rose-Jones
Coffs Harbor, New South Wales, Australia

 

snow erasing
the garden's features—
teaching my dad to shave

Mathew Spano
Hillsborough, New Jersey
 
 

unilingual
I study the expressions
in a wren's eye

Merrill Ann Gonzales
Dayville, Connecticut

 

first cold day
the cat kneads
a patch of sunlight

Lauren Mayhew
Somerville, Massachusetts

 

first flakes
discovering my childhood
in a box

Dietmar Tauchner
Puchberg, Austria

 

Orion's belt
my words byte to byte
to another continent

Deb Baker
Concord, New Hampshire

 

after the funeral
snow covers
the dark places

Jerry Dreesen
Arcadia, Indiana
