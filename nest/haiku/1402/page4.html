piano practice
leaves swirl
allegro con brio

Roberta Beary
Washington, District of Columbia

 

scudding moon
a starfish holds fast
to the hull

Lorin Ford
Melbourne, Victoria, Australia

 

neck
& neck—
geese

Alan S. Bridges
Littleton, Massachusetts

 

first names
no longer in fashion
weathered headstones

John Soules
Wingham, Ontario, Canada

 

between roots and branches
centuries
and a child's swing

Seánan Forbes
London, England

 
 
 

autumn wind
I take what I want
of the apples

Gregory Hopkins
Weaver, Alabama

 

October fest
too much oom-pah-pah
in the mustard

Adelaide B. Shaw
Millbrook, New York

 

leaves in the wind . . .
this time
I let him go

Naia
Temecula, California

 

godless month
a douible rainbow
spans the mourners

Berenice Mortimer
Westlock, Alberta, Canada

 

first snow
cascading through cedar
a woodpecker's call

Margaret Dornaus
Ozark, Arkansas
