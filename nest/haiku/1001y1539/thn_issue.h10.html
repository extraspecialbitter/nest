October light
            I open my ribs
      to pray

Randy Brooks

 

migrating birds
my father sends the next son
on his way

Glenn G. Coats

 

autumn night —
a stranger paints her lips
before the next stop

Lenard D. Moore

 

billowing skirts
by a soldier’s grave
autumn deepens

Chris Bays

 

carillon bells
a wedge of cranes
flying south

Allan Burns

 
 
 

amber light
the time it takes
a leaf to fall

Susan Constable

 

autumn deepens —
the corn maze open
to the cows

Connie Donleycott

 

All Saints’ Day —
under the sheets
a ghost hides her stash

David Giacalone

 

Remembrance Day
traffic sounds crisscross
the bagpipes

Laryalee Fraser

 

that grassy knoll
pigeons explode
into flight

Kay Grimnes
