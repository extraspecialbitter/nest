Memorial Day —
the mockingbird
repeats his song

Ruth Holzer

 

taking the short way
the toes of her shoes
damp with dew

Greg Piko

 

wind surfing
gulls hover
over the wipeouts

William Cullen, Jr.

 

as girls we watched
heat lightning across the bay —
only now the thunder

Helen Russell

 

blinding sunshine —
a stranger with the smile
of an old friend

Valeria Simonova-Cecon

 
 
 

under the rainbow
a hundred cows with
one expression

Peter Yovu

 

distant thunder
my daughters’ laughter
on the carousel

Matthew Cariello

 

Milky Way
a stream of termites
from the woodpile

Lorin Ford

 

       sky of no clouds
sand from the shaman’s hand
      conjures a rainbow

Frank K. Robinson

 

fishing
with dad again
the ripples

Stephen Peters
