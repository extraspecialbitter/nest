new motorcycle
we pass through the scent
of someone’s dinner

Connie Donleycott

 

sun-baked grasslands
the color in the wings
of blackbirds

John Barlow

 

midnight —
the last firecracker
fizzles

Bonnie Stepenoff

 

Romanian village
when the dog has passed
the rooster crows

Bruce Ross

 

autumn rain
she lights her cigarette
from his

Bill Kenney

 
 
 

dusty hike
a snail also crossing
the dry stream

Elizabeth Howard

 

so dry —
the chaparral partly woven
into my socks

Brent Partridge

 

the ragged bark
of a paper birch
his death day

Joann Klontz

 

mother mends her shawl
with tobacco twine . . .
curing season

Curtis Dunlap

 

crescent moon —
the street magician’s trick
finally revealed

Andrea Cecon
