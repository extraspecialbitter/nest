autumn dusk
I wave to a girl

Dietmar Tauchner

 

first time up this creek
the dragonflies
won’t keep still

paul m.

 

crescent moon
the beaver shreds
a lotus leaf

Berenice Mortimer

 

moonlit grass
a farmer whistles catfish
to the surface

Glenn G. Coats

 

our locked gate —
earth stops the falling
leaves

Gary Hotham

 
 
 

mushrooms tilting
into each other —
misty rain

Cindy Zackowitz

 

a place under
the willow tree —
autumn rain

Duro Jaiye

 

autumn mist
in the beggar’s hand —
his empty stare

Terry O’Connor

 

autumn evening
her seventh rejection
of my proposal

Hrushikesh Oka

 

early frost
a scatter of termites
from tonight’s log

Nathalie Buckland
