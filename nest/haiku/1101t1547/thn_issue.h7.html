end of winter
the flannel sheets
worn smooth

Pat Tompkins

 

spring cleaning
our cat curled up
in the give-away box

Lori Olson Randall

 

downpour . . .
two blackbirds bathing
in cherry blossoms

Claudia Brefeld

 

bullfrog eyes
half out
dusk moon

Grant D. Savage

 

carpooling —
I discover my colleague’s
passion for lilies

André Surridge

 
 
 

my teenager
folding paper samurai
spring wind

Cherie Hunter Day

 

gathering clouds
the rusty tractor
in a deep rut

Bob Lucky

 

soap bubbles
from a child’s wand . . .
spring breeze

R. Tracy Roberts

 

long-stay ward
a forged signature
on the Mother’s Day card

Clare McCotter

 

Quaker meeting
a breeze passing
from tree to tree

Gregory Hopkins
