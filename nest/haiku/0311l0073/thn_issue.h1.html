autumn stillness
a magpie shakes seeds
from the torch weed

an'ya

 

family reunion
an assortment of onions
in the stew

Nancy Stewart Smith

 

deep autumn–
the last jigsaw piece
snaps into place

Kathy Lippard Cobb

 

winter pasture
the gray mare favors
one leg

Carolyn Hall

 

ice floes–
the wing of a gull
brushes a star

Sonia Christina Coman

 
 
 

church bells–
a dragonfly
flying low

Fay Aoyagi

 

getting dark . . .
grandpa searches too long
for the North Star

Edith Ellwood

 

the deep curve
of each narrow leaf
first snowfall

Elizabeth St Jacques

 
wildfire haze
between the icebergs
blue wind
Cindy Zackowitz

 

evening shadows
a mouse darts
from the fireplace

Darrell Byrd
