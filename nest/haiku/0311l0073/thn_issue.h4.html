reddening sky
the car park empties
gull by gull

Steve Dolphy

 

the shelter of a tree–
neither of us knows
the other's name

paul m.

 

after the rain
the field's clean shine:
watermelons

Elizabeth St Jacques

 

humid night
a small child's crying
wavers to a stop

Alison Williams

 

summer twilight
the strolling path dips
into coolness

Christopher Patchel

 
 
 

steady summer rain . . .
the gull takes a few short steps
in the puddle

Bruce Ross

 

on the beach,
the tracks of two
lounge chairs

John Stevenson

 

steamed up window
with long oblique lines
a child draws the rain

Ljubomir Dragovic'

 
the indignant cat
has turned its back on me;
ticking clock
Debra Woolard Bender

 

humid night
the scarecrow engulfed
in grapevines
