sunlit meadow —
       fritillaries
       just frittering

Emily Romano

 

a lizard tail
released in my pinched fingers
the scent of thyme

Katherine Cudney

 

the pregnant woman
shifts on the bleachers . . .
extra innings

Deborah Baker

 

scent of roses —
the healer’s hands
grow warmer

George Dorsty

 

dogday night
one bug zap
at a time

Christopher Patchel

 
 
 

the bride
on her way to the groom
scent of rose

Gillena Cox

 

blistering heat
a child frightens me
with a rubber spider

Angelee Deodhar

 

street fair
colors from the suncatcher
touch the mime

Connie Donleycott

 

fiery sky
swifts spiral
into a chimney

Sabine Miller

 

hot night —
the orchestration of crickets
and tinkling ice

Adelaide Shaw
