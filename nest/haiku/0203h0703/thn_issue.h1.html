chilly wind–
a marmot whistles
from the moraine

Darold D. Braida

 

winter morning sun
         on yellow stucco–
the church bells of Florence

Randal Johnson

 

cracked ice
the eagle's tail feathers
spread wide apart

Cindy Zackowitz

 

black ice
a snow swirl from the lake's edge
in slow motion

Marjorie Buettner

 

winter sunset
high in the willow
an egret

Kay F. Anderson

 

 
 

cold clear night–
after the candle flickers out
blue moonlight

Elizabeth St Jacques

 

taking the stairs
two at a time–
snowlight on the ceiling

Dee Evetts

 

winter barnyard
cows in the sunlight
sheep in the shade

Joann Klontz

 

winter bee
working a burial wreath
the twenty-third psalm
Nina Wicker

 

Quaker meeting
through stovepipe-heated air
the world shimmers

Ruth Yarrow
