paul m.

John Stevenson

Randal Johnson

Carol O'Dell

Cyril Childs

Leatrice Lifshitz

November night
with the sound of pelting rain
the barking dog

Elizabeth St Jacques

 

reflection of a candle
flame in the glass
with cider
Tom Clausen

 

no sign
where two roads meet
iris blooms

paul m.

 

offshore birds
a flock swerves and vanishes
in the glitter of waves

Randal Johnson

 

beach of shells
a fisherman casts
into the sunset

Cyril Childs

 
 
 

dark waters crest
      tied to an old oak
      a wooden casket

Nina A. Wicker

 

that four o'clock bird–
strangers
we share the dawn

Leatrice Lifshitz

 

cloverleaf exit
a swell of swallows
in twilight

John Stevenson

 
the kite soars
to the end of the string
indigo sky
Carol O'Dell

 

sunset–
the butterfly and I
just in time

Leatrice Lifshitz
