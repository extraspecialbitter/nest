Debra Woolard Bender

Carlos Colón

William Cullen, Jr.

Robert Major

Stephen Amor

Ljubinka Tosic

Mark Brooks

Lenard D. Moore

Charles Trumbull

Penelope Greenwell

year's end
this unfinished
poem

Debra Woolard Bender

 

the mourning ends–
walking out
into new sunlight

William Cullen, Jr.

 

approaching rain
a second wren tips
the tall thistle

Stephen Amor

 

garden path–
the child sorts good rocks
from bad rocks

Mark Brooks

 

line drive to center
all faces turn toward
the sunflower field

Charles Trumbull

 
 
 

January 1st
fresh grave being covered
by snow

Carlos Colón

 

two red cedars
joined in their early years–
grown apart with age

Robert Major

 

walking the old paths
     I am again
a child!

Ljubinka Tosic

 
red dust–
a little boy sprints the bases
between innings
Lenard D. Moore

 

every now and then
he swats at a fly
midday heat

Penelope Greenwell
