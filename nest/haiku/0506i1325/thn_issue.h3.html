cat’s faint tread —
the light
before sunrise

Barry George

 

first day of school
the Latin teacher
coated in chalk

John S. O’Connor

 

continent’s edge —
a mound of jellyfish
the color of twilight

D. Claire Gallagher

 

shooting star —
a night bird
cries out

George Dorsty

 

roaring sea —
blue anemones
along the path

Odd G. Aksnes

 
 
 

meditation hour
the rain
changing rhythm

Ion Codrescu

 

a strange language
these querulous crows
and no peace     anywhere

Elizabeth Searle Lamb

 

full moon —
a shadow under the curl
of each wave

Adelaide Shaw

 

morning wind
shadows of gulls
skim the dunes

Anita Wintz

 

breakwater rocks —
where I left off yesterday
the morning tide

paul m.
