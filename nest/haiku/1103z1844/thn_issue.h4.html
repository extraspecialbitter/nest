hundreds of gulls
feeding their chicks
he points at the dead one

Greg Piko

 

first warm day —
the woodpecker works
a shiny stump

Cindy Zackowitz

 

dark hallway
an explosion
of cut lilac

Hilary Tann

 

muddy gloves
a colourful bunch
of empty flower pots

Garry Eaton

catching dawn

Shae Davidson

in the same hollow

Greg Piko

on the shoulder —
a raindrop

Walter Franceschi

Mathew Spano

 

night rain
the bedroom fills
with memories

Dietmar Tauchner

 

we let
   our secrets out
      empty nest
