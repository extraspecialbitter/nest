first night out
on their own . . .
my seedlings

Laryalee Fraser

 

spring breeze —
a mosquito in the sap
of a beaver stump

Cindy Zackowitz

 

day after Memorial Day
a rolling trashcan filled
with flags

Carlos Colón

 

field stones
the broadness
of the farmer’s back

Chad Lee Robinson

 

fragile truce —
beet seedlings
in a crooked row

Carolyn Hall

 
 
 

milkmoon —
my daughter nurses
her plastic doll

Raquel D. Bailey

 

spring hail
the horse rolls its tongue
over the fencepost

Susan Constable

 

market stroll —
rotating a coin
in my pocket

Jacek Margolak

 

summer solstice
the long shadow cast
by a plastic T-Rex

Joyce Clement

 

swimmers
at the middle of the lake
graduation night

Marcus Larsson
