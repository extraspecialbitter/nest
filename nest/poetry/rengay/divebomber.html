Divebomber

Rengay with a communication topic written May 24, 1999 in the serenity chat room.

  • Christopher Herold
  • Alex Benedict 
divebomber
the hummingbird protects its nest
...from me?
                     Christopher

coastal antenna farm
whales and submarines offshore
                     Alex

praying mantis
no warning whatsoever
for the poor fellow
                     Christopher

pots stacked high
the crabber sets his course
for home
                     Alex
                     
except for mating cats
no one on the widow's walk
                     Christopher

fluttering
in the sunlight
a spider's web
                     Alex
